Coaching at Hampton in Arden Tennis Club

Head Coach - Ian Poole

Our head coach, Ian Poole, is a level 4 CCA Performance coach. Ian, together with his assistant coaches, runs a comprehensive mini tennis programme for children from as young as 3 years of age. Beginners of all ages are welcome. We also provide adult group lessons on Monday, Thursday, Friday and Saturday mornings, as well as the option to book individual or group lessons at a time to suit you.

Ian is supported in his coaching activities at Hampton in Arden by a team of qualified coaches.

Meet our Coaches

Junior Group Coaching

Hampton in Arden Tennis Club has an extensive programme of coaching for juniors from ages 3 to 18 years old. LTA-qualified staff provide structure (and fun!) to all juniors receiving coaching. Term-time coaching is supplemented with half-term and school holiday time tennis and multi-sport clubs.

Find out more »

LTA Mini-Tennis

Hampton in arden Tennis Club is an accredited LTA Mini-Tennis training centre, teaching the four stages of Mini-Tennis from tots at ages 3-5, up through Mini-Tennis Red (ages 5-8), Mini-Tennis Orange (ages 8 and 9) to Mini-Tennis Green (age 10).

Learn more »

Adult Coaching

Hampton in Arden Tennis Club also provides structured coaching for all levels of adult playing capability, both during the daytimes and at weekends. New Cardio tennis sessions are also provided for those whose goals are fitness and tennis skills.

More details »

One-to-One Coaching

The coaching staff at Hampton in Arden Tennis Club are also available for one-to-one coaching sessions for all ages and abilities, whether you want to catch up with others in your group coaching or need to move on from group coaching activitiies to perfect individual skills.
