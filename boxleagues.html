Box Leagues at Hampton

Singles Box Leagues were first introduced for 2013 to encourage more members to play more tennis. Catering for a variety of playing standards over 8 leagues, these leagues generally run over a period of 2 months.

Additionally, the daytime doubles box league (started in the autumn of 2014) will run over the same period as the above singles box leagues. Enjoy!

The draw for each of the current box leagues is available by clicjking on the appropriate information button. Each league generally consists of 6 or 7 players/pairs which means 5 or 6 matches to play over the next two months. Each match should take about one hour.

If you are not in either of the current box leagues and want to be included in subsequent box leagues, just write your name on the appropriate sheet in the clubhouse before the end of this league. Please don't forget that all playing standards are welcome and encouraged to enter so we will try to put new players into the leagues at the right ability level.

Recording Results

All scores for singles or doubles box leagues are to be recorded via the Leagues option of the Online Booking System (that you use to book courts). Please do NOT write your results onto the paper copy of the leagues shown in the clubhouse - results recorded here will NOT be entered into the online system.

Rules

Each match consists of 12 games. So, for example, if the score is 10-2, the score to record online would be 10-2 (the online system adds the additrional 2 points per player so you dont have to). Please record your score online next to your name in your opponent's column and vice versa. At the end of this league the total scores for each person are used to determine their ranking for each league. The two highest ranked players/pairs will move up a league, and the two lowest ranked players down a league and the next set of box leagues will then be set up.
