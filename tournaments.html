Tournaments at Hampton

Annual Adult Tournament

Hampton runs one internal adult tournament each season, usually commencing in late April/early May. Existing members are invited to opt-in to whichever tournaments they wish to enter near membership renewal time; for new joiners, you are asked to specify your tournament choices online as well. Any member can participate. Several titles are contested and these were (for the 2017 season):

  • Mens: Singles, Doubles, Singles Plate, Veterans Doubles (two age bands)
  • Ladies: Singles, Doubles, Singles Plate, Veterans Doubles (two age bands)
  • Mixed: Doubles, Challenger Doubles

There are two finals days for these tournaments - a summer finals day which is usually held in late June / early July and an autumn finals day which is usually held in September/October.

Junior Tournaments

A variety of tournaments are run throughout the year for our junior members. In 2017, they had two major annual tournaments: a junior tournament run sometime during June with their finals day on the same day as the adult summer finals day, and a Mini-Tennis tournament and finals day in October.
