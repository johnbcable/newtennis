Playing Tennis at Hampton

Choose what type of playing will best suit you

  • Social Tennis

    We run 2 weekday evening sessions (Tues,Fri), a Saturday afternoon family-oriented session and Sunday mornings (from 10:00am). The social sessions run all year round in all but the worst weathers.

  • Box Leagues

    Within the club, we offer competitive singles and doubles box leagues. These are a great way for you to vary who you play against and improve your singles and doubles match play.

  • Tournaments

    We organise tournaments for all age groups and standards throughout the year. In the main tournament there are 11 adult and 6 junior titles contested each year - something for everyone.

  • Coventry League

    Why not try out for one of our Coventry League tennis teams? We have 5 mens and 3 ladies teams that compete during the league season (late April to early August) in the Coventry and District Lawn Tennis League

Other Playing Opportunities

  • Aegon Team Tennis

    You are playing to reasonable standard but would prefer more singles play? Aegon Team Tennis may be for you.

    Contact aegonteamtennis@hamptontennis.org.uk for further information.

  • Coaching

    Having coaching lessons is a great way to sharpen up your game, and to meet other members of the club of all ages. It's not just drills and more drills - your coach will take you through a coaching route to benefit your game. Choose from group sessions or individual tuition - whatever works for you: we've got it covered!
