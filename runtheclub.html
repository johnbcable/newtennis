Social Events

Have you an idea for a social event that the tennis club could put on? Would you like to help with organising and running any new or existing social events?

  • coordinate Sports Club sponsors
  • help develop Sports Club marketing collateral
  • help produce posters / leaflets
  • help run social media communications (Twitter, Facebook etc)
  • help coordinate publicity for the local press
  • help organise tennis Finals Day, incl. noting down scores
  • help organise Family Wimbledon trip (11th and 12th July)
  • help organise Friday and weekend socials e.g. BBQ's

Administration

We are always looking for volunteers to help with the administration aspects of running the club effectively for the benefit of its members. Would you like to help?

  • keeping stony court surrounds weeded
  • keeping BBQ clean and tidy
  • sweeping patio area twice a month
  • organise club-branded clothing - buying + selling
  • help minimise clubhouse overheads e.g. insurance, electricity, gas etc
  • bar and clubhouse accounting
  • keeping club First Aid box adequately stocked
  • updating LTA database of Hampton British Tennis members
  • help to chase late payments - membership fees, coaching fees etc
  • volunteer to be club representative on the Coventry Lawn Tennis League committee

Arden 9

We host the annual Arden 9-Mile Road Race. The event generates much needed revenue for the club. Can you spare some time on the morning of Sunday 21st June, 2015? More »

  • recruit Arden 9 marshals
  • perpeare on-the-day briefings for marshals
  • be a marshal or other helper on the day of the race
  • help run the on-the-day registration desk(s)
  • help with the on-the-day hospitality e.g. teas, cakes etc
  • on-the-day accounting and initial floats
  • tidy up Sports Club and grounds after the event
