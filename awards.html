Our Awards

  • Coventry League Awards

    Hampton league teams have been doing well in their local (Coventry) league: the Mens 1st Team topped the Premier Division for the third year in a row; the Mens 3rd Team and the Ladies 1st Team also topped their divisions.

  • Young Volunteer of the Year

    Sam Hiskett, one of our coaching team here at Hampton, was awarded the Young Volunteer of the Year award by Warwickshire LTA at their awards dinner in 2014

  • Tennis Clubmark

    Hampton-In-Arden Tennis Club received official Tennis Clubmark accreditation on 29 September, 2008. This national award recognizes years of hard work that has been put into developing best practice policies in running our club.

  • Warwickshire LTA Club of the Year 2007

    Hampton-in-Arden Tennis Club was chosen as the Club of the Year by the Warwickshire Lawn Tennis Association in 2007. This was announced at an awards ceremony sponsored by Jobson James which was held on Friday 18 January, 2008 at the Botanical Gardens in Edgbaston.
