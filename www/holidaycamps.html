Holiday Clubs

The coaching staff at Hampton regularly run tennis and multi-sport camps during the half-term school holidays and often at Christmas, Easter and during the school summer holidays.

Easter 2019

Easter tennis camps will run on :–

  • Monday 15th, Tuesday 16thand Wednesday 17th April, and
  • Tuesday 23rd and Wednesday 24th April

The above camp is aimed at children aged 6 and over. Camps all run from 9.30am to 3.30pm.

Early drop offs from 8.30 am. Remember to bring a drink and a packed lunch.

Tots Camps

In addition, we are offering half day camps this Easter holiday as follows:-

  • Monday 15th, Tuesday 16thand Wednesday 17th April, and
  • Tuesday 23rd and Wednesday 24th April

These camps are aimed at children aged under 6. Each camp will run from 9.30am to 12.30.

For any of the above camps, please do remember to bring a drink and a packed lunch.

Please note that booking AND pre-payment are ESSENTIAL for all camps so that we can ensure we have adequate staff available. Bookings will only be accepted with an online transfer or cheque/cash payment to Ian.

If paying on the day, the tennis camps and tots camps will cost more and we CANNOT guarantee that we will be able to accommodate your child.

Interested? Please email ianpoole23@yahoo.co.uk (with brief details of who will attend and when) to secure your place as early as possible. Ian will provide details of costs.

Once booked, camps are non-refundable. Numbers on the camps are limited per day.

Please Remember: bookings are ONLY secured with payment.
