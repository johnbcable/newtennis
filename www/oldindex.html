Welcome to Hampton in Arden Tennis Club

Your family-friendly tennis club

  • Join Us

    We hope you enjoyed the recent 4 week Rusty Rackets course that ran from 30th April. Hopefully this has encouraged you to carry on and join the club.

  • Challenger Tournament 2019

    Our popular Challenger Tournament started on 1st May 2019. Finals day will be on Saturday 29th June 2019 during the Hampton Festival when many other activities will take place alongside some great tennis.

  • Coventry League Tennis

    The Coventry League tennis season has now started. Why not pop along to a home match to see how your ladies and mens teams are doing representing the club ... and enjoy a visit to the bar while you are there!

  • Coaching

    We run a comprehensive mini tennis programme for children from as young as 3 years of age. Beginners of all ages are welcome. We also provide adult group lessons on Monday, Thursday, Friday and Saturday mornings, as well as the option to book individual or group lessons at a time to suit you.

  • Holiday Clubs

    Our popular tennis and multi-sport camps run during most school holidays and are open to both members and non-members. Camps normally run from 9.30am to 3.30pm with early drop-off available.

  • Social Tennis

    Members can attend as many social tennis sessions as they wish from the variety available on weekday evenings and at weekends at no extra cost except for a contribution to lighting costs if required. The social sessions run all year round in all but the worst weathers.

Get More Involved

Improve your game - help the club!

  • League Teams

    Why not try out for one of our Coventry League tennis teams? We have 5 mens and 3 ladies teams that compete during the league season (late April to early August) in the Coventry and District Lawn Tennis League

  • Box Leagues

    Within the club, we offer competitive singles and doubles box leagues. These are a great way for you to vary who you play against and improve your match play.

  • Tournaments

    We organise tournaments for all age groups and standards throughout the year. In the main tournament there are 11 adult and 6 junior titles contested each year - something for everyone.

  • Help run the club

    Could you help us run the club? We are always pleased for volunteers to help in variety of ways: with teams and competitions, with social events and with a variety of administration tasks.

Join Us

There are many benefits to becoming a member of Hampton-in-Arden Tennis Club, whether you are an adult or a child. These include preferential pricing on certain activities, booking courts and web access to club communications. See what we have to offer - click Join Us now!
