Tournaments at Hampton

Annual Adult Tournaments 2019

Challenger Tournament

We will again be running our popular Challenger Tournament which will start on 1st May 2019. Finals day will be on Saturday 29th June 2019 during the Hampton Festival when many other activities will take place alongside some great tennis. Titles contested for in the Challenger Tournament are:

  • Mens: Singles, Doubles
  • Ladies: Singles, Doubles
  • Mixed: Doubles

N.B. For any of the doubles titles, you may enter with a partner or we can find one for you.

To be eligible to participate in he Challenger Tournament:

  1. You must be a fully paid up member
  2. You must be available to play on finals day
  3. Mens first to fourth team players are excluded
  4. Ladies first team players are excluded

All matches will be the best of 3 sets with the 3rd set (if required) played as a championship tie break.

1st round losers of the Men's and Ladies singles will automatically go into a plate competition.

To enter please e-mail tournaments@hamptontennis.org.uka> stating which tournaments (excluding plate) you wish to enter by 19th April.

Open Tournament

Now that we've just about recovered from a very hot Challenger Finals Day we're thinking about the Open Tournament, the final of which will take place on Saturday 5th October, when it'll be much cooler and hopefully dry!

In the Open Tournament the following titles are up for grabs:

  • Men's Singles*, Doubles, Vets Doubles (over 40's) and Super Vets Doubles (over 60's)
  • Ladies Singles**, Doubles, Vets Doubles (over 40's) and Super Vets Doubles (over 60's)
  • Mixed Doubles, Mixed Plate***

* – first round losers will automatically go into the Men's Singles Plate tournament

** – first round losers will automatically go into the Ladies Singles Plate tournament

*** – partners will be allocated by the tournament directors on a seeded basis.

Entry Criteria for the Open Tournament

  • You must be a fully paid up member. You cannot enter if you are a daytime member only.
  • You must be available to play on Finals Day - Saturday 5th October

To enter please e-mail tournaments@hamptontennis.org.uk stating which tournaments you wish to enter by 14th July.

If you have any questions about the tournaments please contact the tournament directors –

  • Men's – Sav Gaspar – 07786 872011
  • Ladies – Ruth Fennell – 07790 854430
