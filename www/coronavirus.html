Corona Virus (COVID 19)

Update from 23 rd March

Dear member, as you will have seen the Government put extra social restrictions in place this evening. In accordance with this, the Sports Club board will be making the tennis courts unavailable for at least the next 3 weeks. We will review this policy as the current situation evolves.

We would also like to encourage members who are planning to utilise the boules pitches to follow the guidelines fully and avoid any such activity unless it is solely alone or with a member of your own household.

If in doubt please avoid using this shared facility for the good of your fellow residents.

Stay safe

Coaching Activities

In light of the Corona virus, the tennis club (with the support of the coaching staff) have decided to suspend all group coaching sessions until further notice. (Issued 23 March 2020)

The coaching staff will continue to offer 1:1 coaching sessions. Please continue to support the coaching staff by carrying on with your 1:1 lessons.
