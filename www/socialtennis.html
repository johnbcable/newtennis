Social Tennis at Hampton in Arden Tennis Club

The club has 4 main social tennis sessions each week. Primarily for adult members, the sessions give you a chance to meet up with other tennis members outside of league tennis or club tournaments. The days/times when 3 of the tennis courts are given over for social tennis are shown below. The format is doubles played in a friendly atmosphere - but dont think your tennis wont be challenged!

  • Tuesday
  • 8.00pm to 10.00pm
  • Members Only
  • Friday
  • 8.00pm to 10.00pm
  • Members Only
  • Saturday
  • 3.00pm to 5.00pm
  • Members Only
  • Sunday
  • 10.30am to 12.30am
  • Members Only

Saturday Family Social tennis

Saturday Social Tennis runs each Saturday afternoon. The tennis sessions start at 3pm and last until aprox. 5pm.

The junior social session is open to members aged 6 and over and there is no fee. A coach will be present to organise fun games and drills for the children.

The adult social session runs at the same time as the junior social and is open to players of all standards.

Friay morning doubles

This is very popular and the 10-11am slot on a Friday morning gets filled very quickly but if there is enough interest then people can still play from 11-12. If you are interested in playing please contact social@hamptontennis.org.uk. We can find you a partner if you don't have one.

Pop Up Social Tennis

Contact social@hamptontennis.org.uk if you want to be added to the Whatsapp group. This is open to anyone that wants to play day time social tennis on an ad hoc basis.

Social Tournaments

From time to time, the club organises competitive social tournaments designed to get more of our membership playing. For some years, for example, we ran a MAXI-Tennis tournament at the same time as the 6-Nations rugby, where teams were named after the 6 nations rugby sides.

Join Us

There are many benefits to becoming a member of Hampton-in-Arden Tennis Club, whether you are an adult or a child. These include preferential pricing on certain activities, booking courts and web access to club communications. See what we have to offer - click Join Us now!
