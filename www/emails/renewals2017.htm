Your tournament entry preferences

As for 2016, we are holding the Challenger and Junior Finals Day in early July – on Saturday July 1st. The Open and Veterans Finals Day will be held on Saturday September 30th. A Mini-Tennis tournament and Finals Day will be on Saturday October 14th.

The tournaments will take the same format as last year except that this year we are holding two veterans doubles tournaments at 45+ and 60+ instead of three (40+, 50+ and 60+) held last year. If you enter more than one doubles tournament you must select a different partner for each tournament.

Challenger events to be a qualifier for the Open Singles, Doubles and Mixed Doubles titles with 2 finalists from each event qualifying into the main tournament.

If you are in the top four mens or top two ladies Coventry league squads or you have played in the top singles box league in 2016 you will be ineligible for the Challenger tournament and will therefore receive a bye into the main tournament draw.

Your tournament choices will need to be selected during the membership renewals (or joining) process.
