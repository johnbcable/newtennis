Sunday 19 June 2011

10.30am start

To everyone registered on Runners World for the 2011 ASICS Arden9 mile road race:

Thank you very much for entering the 2011 race. Please can you read the race instructions before coming, using the link to our website shown below.

Please note that your race number needs to be collected from registration before the race so please do arrive in good time. The queues for collecting your race number will be separate from the queues for those who wish to pay on the day to ensure that you do not have a long wait to collect your number.

The presentation ceremony will take place at 12 noon.

Good luck!

For more information, please visit our web site http://arden9.org.uk:

Thank you - and we look forward to seeing you on June 19th.
