Wimbledon - Men's Finals BBQ!

Sunday 6 July, 2003
12:30 pm onwards - BBQ
2:00 pm The Match Starts

Have you been following Wimbledon? Would you like to make a social event of watching the men’s final?  Then why not join us for a bite to eat and a drink at the club? You can either watch the match on the big screen television, play some social tennis yourself, or do both!

Tickets cost £5 for adults and £3 for children and include:

  • Burger/hot dog (meat purchased from a reputable local butcher)
  • Strawberries and cream
  • Punch or soft drink

Friends/relatives of members are also welcome.
