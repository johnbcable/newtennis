Hampton-In-Arden Tennis Club

Not a runner?

The Knowle Fun Run very much lives up to its name - it is a primarily social occasion where funds can be raised for a variety of charitable causes by being active. If you are not a runner, then all the more incentive for you to get out and particpiate - as a side benefit, you will probably attract good sponsorship as running is so unusual for you!

The course in recent years has been run over two circuits of two and a half miles each giving plenty of opportunity for rests and, if necessary, early bail out. The addede incentive this year is the extra sponsorship offered to Hampton Sports Club runners (see below) which will help immensely in bossting the amount of money we can donate top our chosen charities this year.

Karen Finch

Knowle Fun Run - Sports Club Team

Call for participants

Last year, 44 runners entered from Hampton - and were the largest team entry in this event. This year, funds raised by the team will be divided between St Mary`s Hospice, Selly Oak and the Hampton Recreational Trust. The Lillie Johnson Charitable Trust has kindly pledged £50 per runner. The Entry Form is in the main entrance of the Hampton in Arden Sports Club. £6.00 entry for Juniors and Secondary age, £12.00 for adults and veterans.

Please hand in fees to either Sue Kiely at 3 Meriden Rd (01675 443989) or Karen Finch at 9 Station Rd (01675 443160), or behind the Sports Club bar. Tee shirt colour will be pillar box red.
