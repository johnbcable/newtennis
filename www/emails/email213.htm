Hampton-In-Arden Tennis Club


Tots and Red Mini-Tennis Event
Sunday 13 July


Many thanks to all the children and grown-ups who supported the tots and red mini-tennis event on Sunday 13 July. There was a very high turnout including 16 of the more advanced players who took part in a tournament. Congratulations to Lexi Bouvier who was the winner and to Tom Lord who was runner up.

Thanks also to the volunteers without whose help the event could not have taken place and also to our coaches Ian and Dave for their hard work on court.

Best wishes to all our juniors for their future tennis playing - it is great that you are playing at such a young age.

Junior Tennis Committee
