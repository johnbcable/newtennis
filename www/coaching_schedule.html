Current Junior Coaching Schedule

To join a junior coaching programme, please download and print a booking form, complete it and hand it to your coach (or send it to him at the address below) complete with your fee prior to starting your course.

Apply Now

The summer 2019 coaching programme starts on Monday 29th April and runs to Saturday 20th July (excluding half term, week beginning 25th May).

Tots Tennis - (Ages 3-5)

Co-ordination fun based activities introducing skills with the racket to develop the basic fundamentals of the game.

  • Sat 0900 - 0930
  • Sat 0930 - 1000

Mini-Tennis Red - (Ages 5-8)

Scaled down Court, rackets etc.
Sponge indoor felt outdoor.
Beginning to learn the basic technical shapes and tactics of the game.
(The lower the number in brackets the more advanced the group. Some mini tennis red will undertake some mini orange lessons)

  • Mon 1600 - 1700
  • Thu 1545 - 1630 (2 classes - beginner and improver)
  • Fri 1545 - 1630 (2 classes - beginner and improver)
  • Sat 0900 - 0945

Mini-Tennis Orange - (Ages 8/9)

Smaller than full court, transition balls allowing more time and less height off the bounce. Beginning to introduce more shape and spin to technique. Bigger courts allow the introduction of a wider choice of tactics.

  • Mon 1600 - 1700
  • Tue 1630 - 1715
  • Wed 1545 - 1630
  • Wed 1545 - 1630

Girls Only

Cpaching targetted towards our junior female members

  • Tue 1630 - 1715
  • Fri 1700 - 1800 Girls Improver

Full Ball

Full court and normal yellow tennis balls, technical and tactical appropriate to the needs of the group.

  • Mon 1700 - 1800 (Under 14)
  • Wed 1545 - 1630 Full Ball Junior
  • Fri 1800 - 1900 Under 16 Development Full Ball

Under 18 and Invite-Only Squads

  • Tue 1800 - 1900 U18 Elite (Invite only)
  • Fri 1900 - 2000 U18 Elite
