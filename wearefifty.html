Hampton Tennis Club is Fifty in 2016!

Hampton in Arden Tennis Club was first established in 1966 so now, in 2016, we are celebrating our fiftieth year. We have souvenirs, social occasions and sporting activities for you - read on!

Souvenir Merchandise

To commemorate the fiftieth year, we have commissioned some souvenir mementoes that you can purchase.

Pick up a limited edition souvenir mug from behind the bar - only £5 each - bargain! Or go for a souvenir T-shirt starting at £12 (children) and £15 (adults - Unisex).

Social Events

On 11th June, Hampton Tennis Club will host its 50th Anniversary Ball. Only a few tickets left so make sure and get yours as soon as possible!

So don't miss out - please sign up for this special event today!

What's On

We are organising lots of events, shows and things to do over our celebratory weekend of the 10th and 11th June, 2016. Here are some of the activities currently planned:

  • an "Open Mic" session for budding singers and musicians (see below)
  • take a trip down memory lane with our tennis memorabilia display
  • pyjama tennis coaching - 3 sessions on Saturday 11th June
  • Davis Cup generation tennis tournament
  • challenge Sam - 24-hour tennis (fund raising)
  • Kids Disco (see below)
  • food!!! Tennis breakfasts, BBQ and lots more
  • our Anniversary Ball (above)

Open Mic Session

As part of our 50th anniversary celebration extravaganza we’re laying on some live music in the clubhouse at 3pm – 5pm on Saturday 11th June. Some of the members of the band booked for the evening will be performing an acoustic set in the afternoon and that’s where you come in. It’s an open mic session so calling all singers, guitarists, musicians of any kind, all are welcome, come and join us and the stage is yours!

Get involved and get in touch with Nigel Jones to book your slot.

Junior Social Events

As part of the celebrations, junior members have their own "Kids Disco" - adults not allowed! The disco is held on Friday 10th June and will also feature pizza and drinks! Ticket price is £5 per person - get your tickets early - available at the clubhouse bar!

Hampton Village Festival

The annual Hampton Village Festival is also being held on Challenger Finals Day - see what those celebrations have in store to round off your weekend!

Volunteer / Questions

There is a 50th anniversary "committee" but ideas are welcome for other things that would add to the celebrations. If you have a good idea, then let us know. Or if you have a question for the organisers, click on the button to email it to us.

Join Us

You already know there are many benefits to becoming a member of Hampton-in-Arden Tennis Club, whether you are an adult or a child. Invite a friend or family member to join as well - download an application form for them today!
