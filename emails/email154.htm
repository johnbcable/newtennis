JUNIOR SINGLES COMPETITION!

Annual Junior Tennis Tournament

Sunday 14 October 2007, 1 pm onwards

Entry Fee £3

Prizes for winners, sponsored by Widney Sports

All junior members who are capable of full court rallying with full compression tennis balls, are invited to take part in the Annual Junior Tennis Tournament!

This is a great opportunity to put into practice all the skills you have been developing.  So, come and play singles with your friends at the Club!

Medals and trophies for runners up and winners.  Prizes for the winners.

Four categories:

GIRLS, BOYS UNDER 12 (as at 1st January 2007)

GIRLS, BOYS UNDER 16 (as at 1st January 2007)

This event is for MEMBERS ONLY.  If you aren't already a member, please contact Chester Barnes on for details, go onto our website www.hamptontennis.org.uk, or pick up a membership application form from the tennis notice board in the club house.
