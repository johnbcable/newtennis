AUTUMN 2009 JUNIOR COACHING PROGRAMME - STARTING SOON

Thursday 3 September to Saturday 19 December 2009

(excluding half term, week beginning 26 October)

Summer has gone so quickly that it is hard to believe that the autumn term is about to commence. Thursday, Friday and Saturday lessons recommence next week, from 3 September. Other lessons recommence the following week from Monday 7 September.

Early in August an email was sent out regarding the autumn term coaching together with a schedule and a booking form for the junior coaching programme. These documents are attached again, in case you need them.

As already stated, the tennis courses recommence on Thursday 3 September, in line with the start of the new school year. This means that the Thursday, Friday and Saturday lessons will run for 15 weeks, whereas the Mondays, Tuesdays and Wednesdays will run for 14 weeks. You may have already paid the coaching fees, but if not, here is a reminder about the costs:

The costs are as follows:
  Mons, Tues & Weds Thurs, Fris and Sats
1 hour lessons £70 £75
