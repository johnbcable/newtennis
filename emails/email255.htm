Hampton-In-Arden Tennis Club Social Event
 Start of the Tennis Season
Informal Get Together 
Saturday 18 April 2009
from 7.30pm at Hampton Manor
Tickets, £10 to include food

 Both the start of the tennis season together with the completion of the new courts will soon be here.  To get in the mood to play tennis and to catch up with fellow members, we are holding an informal get together at Hampton Manor.
Although we are not having a sit down meal there will be some amuse-bouche (appetisers chosen by the chef) plus hot "bowl" food, which is literally what it sounds like - food served hot from the kitchen in a bowl.  A vegetarian option is available.
Please note that places are limited to 40, so if you are interested please let Chester have a cheque made payable to " Hampton-in-Arden Sports Club"  as soon as possible.  Sorry, but bookings not accompanied by payment cannot be accepted.  Please send your cheque to Chester Barnes, 68 Meriden Road, Hampton-in-Arden, Solihull , B92 0BT .  Any queries please phone Chester or Jayne on
Looking forward to seeing you on the 18th!
