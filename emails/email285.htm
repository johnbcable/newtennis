AUTUMN 2009 JUNIOR COACHING PROGRAMME

Thursday 3 September to Saturday 19 December 2009

(excluding half term, week beginning 26 October)

The summer term has only just ended, but we are already planning for the next term.

Please find attached, a schedule and a booking form for the autumn 2009 junior coaching programme..

All juniors aged 7 or over on 1 May 2009 must be members of the tennis club.  However, attendees who are new to the courses can play for half a term without being members whilst they decide whether tennis at Hampton is for them.  For more information on joining the tennis club, please click here.

Juniors aged under 7 on 1 May 2009 have to be on the club database. There is no charge for this.

The tennis courses recommence on Thursday 3 September, in line with the start of the new school year.  This means that the Thursday, Friday and Saturday lessons will run for 15 weeks, whereas the Mondays, Tuesdays and Wednesdays will run for 14 weeks.

The costs are as follows:
  Mons, Tues & Weds Thurs, Fris and Sats
1 hour lessons £70 £75
45 minutes £56 £60
30 minutes £42 £45
 

In order to encourage prompt payment, which makes administration much easier, we are introducing discounts as shown below for those who pay their termly fee on or before their first lesson.

Chasing payment is time consuming and tedious. There will be a surcharge for late payments not received by the 3rd lesson of term.

  Prompt Payment Discount Late Payment Surcharge
1 hour lessons £6 £6
45 minutes £5 £5
30 minutes £3 £3
 

To assist our planning, if you will not be returning to the courses in September, we would be grateful if you could let us know by replying to this email or by letting Ian know on: 07968 451956

We wish you an enjoyable summer and look forward to seeing you back at lessons in September!
