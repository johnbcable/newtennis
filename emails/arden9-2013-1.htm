Thank you very much for taking part in previous Arden 9 mile road races. The 2013 Arden 9 mile road race will once again be hosted by Knowle & Dorridge Running Club and has also been accepted as part of the 2013 Warwickshire Road Race League.

Online entries only please through Runners World: http://www.runnersworld.co.uk/kdasicsarden9

Entry fee £11 (for UK Athletics affiliated club members) or £13 otherwise.
No credit card fees.
www.arden9.org.uk

Medals will be awarded to all finishers and trophies to category winners.

Please note that 2013 race category prizes will be awarded in line with WRRL competition categories, namely:- Overall (first 3), 35-44, 45-54 and 55+.

Teams comprise 4 runners for Men and 3 runners for Ladies
