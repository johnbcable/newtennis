REMINDER


Under 12's (as at 1 January, 2008)



Annual Junior Singles Tennis Tournament 2008


Sunday 22nd, 1.00 pm Onwards

Entry fee £4 per person


Spectators are welcome to come and support the children - please try to attend. There will be tea and cakes!

All junior members who are capable of full court rallying with full compression tennis balls, are invited to take part in the Annual Junior Tennis Tournament!. Following on from the special "Pink Day" at the DFS Ladies International Tennis Tournament, we would particularly like to see lots of girls entering.

This is a great opportunity to put into practice all the skills you have been developing.  So, come and play singles with your friends  at the Club!

Medals and trophies for runners up and winners.

Two categories

GIRLS UNDER 12 (as at 1st January 2008)
BOYS UNDER 16 (as at 1st January 2008)

This event is for MEMBERS ONLY.  If you aren't already a member, please contact Chester Barnes on 01675 443572 for details, go onto our website www.hamptontennis.org.uk, or pick up a membership application form from the tennis notice board in the club house.
