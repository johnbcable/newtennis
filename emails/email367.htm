Wimbledon Tickets - Draw Results

The results of the draw conducted at the Sports Club on Friday 1st April are shown below. If your name is on the list, you have paid your 2011/12 subscription, are at least 10 years old on June 20th and a BTM member please send a cheque payable to Hampton-in-Arden Sports Club to Wayman Morris and make arrangements to collect the tickets by Saturday April 16th. If you do not wish to take up your allocation, please let Wayman know as soon as possible to help us to find homes quickly for all tickets allocated to the club. Wayman can be contacted on 01675 442071 or at waymanmorris@mail.com. He lives behind the Sports Club in the house with a steep in and out drive at Shadowbrook, Shadowbrook Lane, Hampton-in-Arden, Solihull B92 0DQ

If you do not contact Wayman by Saturday April 16th we will assume that you do not wish to buy the tickets. All tickets not taken up will be offered by email to reserves on Tuesday April 19th and allocated on the basis of the earliest telephone call received by Wayman.

You may nominate a guest of your own choosing to take with you who does not have to be a member of the tennis club, provided that if the named person is under 16 he or she must be accompanied by an adult guest. Please note that under LTA rules the tickets are NOT transferable. This means that if the named member cannot go, the ticket allocation must be returned to the club to be offered to a member drawn on the reserve list in the sequence shown below. Please note that if you do not follow this rule you run the risk of jeopardising the club's future ticket allocation. Under no circumstances should you sell your tickets.

Thank you for your assistance
