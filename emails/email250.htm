Our Thanks, plus

A Special Introductory Offer for Membership

 

 

Whilst the courts are out of action The Hampton Health and Fitness Club is running group training sessions for our older juniors on Saturday mornings from 10.30 to 11.30am.  Matt Ensor, who is a qualified and experienced performance coach at The Hampton, is leading these training sessions which include tennis specific activities.  We would like to thank The Hampton staff for this generous support and encouragement for our juniors.

 

In addition, The Hampton is offering members of our tennis club and parents of our juniors a special introductory offer.  If you join before 30th April 2009 there will be no joining or administration fees to pay. The Hampton will also include a complimentary induction and fitness assessment to help get you started.

 

There is a variety of memberships available, including an adult membership package for £39 per month, which allows full use of the facilities, induction and personal training programme.  The junior membership (for 12-16’s) includes access to a circuit class on a Thursday (6.30pm), along with an induction and personal training programme for £14.95 per month*.

 

The Hampton has recently been refurbished including a new beauty suite, so come and take a look at what is on offer.

 

For more information on this offer, which is available exclusively to our tennis members, or if you would like to arrange a time to come and visit the gym, please contact Gareth or Paul on 01675 443464 or visit the website at www.thehampton.co.uk

 

Our thanks again to The Hampton for their support.

 

 

 

*This offer is only available for members and parents of junior members of Hampton-in-Arden tennis club who are currently non-members of The Hampton Health & Fitness club.  The offer is based upon a minimum membership of 3 months and the offer ends on 30th April 2009.  Memberships are paid for by monthly direct debits and for junior memberships these must be signed by either a parent or guardian.
