Pre-Term Autumn Coaching Reminder

The revised Autumn Term Coaching Programme for Juniors is attached. These courses all run from week beginning 1 September to week beginning 15 December, excluding the half-term week (which is week beginning 27 October). This makes each course a 15-week course. Please note head coach Ian would appreciate all fees due for these autumn term courses to be paid by the start of the autumn term. Also attached is a booking form for you to use when signing-up for these courses.

The revised schedule has taken the opportunity to reorganise some of the group timings. These differ from the schedule previously circulated (on 12 July) as follows:

  1. The Tots tennis group that previously ran on Thursday from 1630-1715 now runs on Wednesdays from 1600-1630,
  2. The Mini-Tennis Red 1 group that previously ran on Tuesday from 1630-1715 is now a Mini-Tennis Orange group,
  3. The Friday 1545-1630 Mini-Tennis Red 2 group is now a Mini-Tennis Red 3 group, and finally
  4. There will be an extra Mini-Tennis Green group running on Fridays from 1630-1715 which will run at the same time as the Mini-Tennis Orange session.

N.B.
All juniors should stay in their existing groups unless Ian has discussed a change. Juniors who already attend an invitation only squad do not need a further invitation to attend.
