Annual Tournament Matches
REMINDER

Last 16 matches not played by Sunday June 17th  - Please note that any last 16 matches which have not been played by Sunday June 17th will automatically be awarded to the first named person or pair in the draw unless you have booked a court to play the match and you have contacted Steve Ray on steve.ray@btinternet.com to let him know when you are playing the match. Please note that there will be limited court space available in the week leading up to finals day so please can you try your best not to delay the competition? You can find the draw on the tennis notice board or by clicking the button on the home page of the web site.

Booking a court  - You can check court availability and book courts by going to www.hamptontennis.org.uk,  clicking on the "Book a Court" link and then logon with your User ID and PIN. If you need help or you have forgotten your User ID or PIN please email support@hamptontennis.org.uk.

Dispensation to play tournament matches on court 4 during social Tennis on Tuesday and Friday evenings  - All four courts are normally reserved for social tennis on Tuesday and Friday evenings from 8pm. In order to help to get all tournament matches played by finals day on July 7th, special dispensation is given in the two weeks leading up to finals day for court 4 to be booked for tournament matches. If you do need to book court 4 please can you book the 6 to 8pm slot first or if that is booked then you can book the 8 to 10pm slot. Please do not book from 7pm to 9pm as this will make it difficult to play two tournament matches during the evening and will also disrupt social tennis for no good reason.

Thank you and regards to you all, Geoff Redfern, Tournament Committee Chairman
