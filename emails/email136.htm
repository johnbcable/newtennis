Club Clothing

 

We are pleased to be able to offer a range of high quality club clothing as shown in the leaflet on the web site.
 
The garments are made of specialist performance sports fabrics.  We are dealing directly with the manufacturer so are able to acquire these items at very reasonable prices.
 
If you are interested and wish to see samples, please speak to our coach, Alan Cutts.  Alternatively, come to Play Tennis Day on Saturday 5 May, when samples will be available.
 
Please note that we will be placing one bulk order after the bank holiday.  Therefore, we need firm orders including cheques by the evening of Sunday 6 May at the latest.
 
The Junior and Main Tennis Committees would like to thank Alan for researching and organising the club clothing.
