Defibrillator training

Following notification at the AGM I confirm that the Sports Club has now purchased a 'state of the art' automated external defibrillator - 'AED'. Our thanks to the 'Sponsored Dieters' who raised over £900 towards the cost and Pete McBride for sourcing the equipment on very competitive terms.
 
Training ( basic life support - BLS and AED ) will be undertaken by West Midlands Ambulance Service and is to take place in the clubhouse on the following dates/times:
 
Sunday 3rd June 9.30am - 1pm
Monday 4th June 6.30pm - 10pm
Thursday 7th June 6.30pm - 10pm
 
We wish to train approximately 25 club members so that in the unfortunate event of an incident there is a good chance that a qualified person is on site. Accordingly all bar staff are to be trained plus 3 individuals nominated by each section.  Volunteers should be physically fit and regular club users.
 
If you would like to volunteer please reply to this email by Saturday 26th May stating which date you would prefer to be trained on.
 
This initiative is part of the National Defibrillation Scheme and our AED is the first to be located within the village.
 
John Eccleston
