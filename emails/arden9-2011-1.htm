Arden 9-Mile Road Race

Sunday 19 June 2011 - 10.30am

We are writing to thank you for taking part in previous Arden 9 races and to invite you to compete once again in the eighth running of the Arden9 race on Sunday 19 June 2011 starting at 10.30am. The race will be run over the same course as previous years.

This year for the first time entries will be accepted ONLY through Runners World:

http://www.runnersworld.co.uk/events/viewevent.asp?sp=&v=2&EN=57760

The entry fee is £12 with a £2 discount if you are a UK Athletics affiliated club member

Every runner finishing the race will receive a medal. Trophies will be awarded to winners of the following categories plus a NEW team category (Teams of 4 runners should be registered on the day of the race).

As in previous years the race is intended for both regular runners and novices. It has been approved by the Midland Counties Athletic Association as a race to be held under the current laws of competition of the Amateur Athletic Association of England and in accordance with the bye-laws of the Midland Counties Athletic Association. The course has been measured as nine miles by the UK Athletics Course Measurement Scheme.



Click on the Runners World logo to register for this race.

Finally, please could we ask you to forward this invitation to your running club secretary with a recommendation to include the Arden9 in your club's race calendar?

Thank you - and we look forward to seeing you again on June 19th.

The ASICS Arden 9 Organisation Team
