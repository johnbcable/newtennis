Summer 2012 Junior Coaching Programme

Monday 23 April to Saturday 21 July 2012

(excluding half term, week beginning 4 June)

The summer term will be starting in just a few weeks and a coaching schedule and booking form are attached.

The courses start on Monday 23rd April and run for 12 weeks. The coaching fees are as follows:

1-hour lessons £66.00
45-minute lessons £54.00
30-minute lessons £41.00

It is helpful in terms of lesson planning and staffing for Ian to know as soon as possible how many children will be booking onto the coaching programme. To this end we are offering a discount of £3 for people who return the booking form and pay promptly for their lessons. The discount may be deducted from the above rates on payments made on or before the first lesson of term.

Please hand deliver payment to Ian or post to Flat 1, Bromford Mere, Warwick Road, Solihull, B92 7AN.
Please make all cheques payable to

Matchpoint Sportz Ltd

Sportz is spelt with a "z".
