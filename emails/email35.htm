Juniors - Playing in Status

Some of the older juniors have reached a very good standard of tennis.  In order to further their advancement and add variety to the tennis of both them and of adult members, some of the juniors are granted permission to play-in at adult sessions.  Selection is based upon assessment of playing ability by the Club coaches.

The chosen juniors are obviously subject to the same court rules as adults.  However, during times of peak court usage, when players have to stand out during mix-in sessions because of a shortage of courts, only two juniors at a time may play in.  In normal circumstances, which two juniors should be the ones to play in must be decided amongst themselves based upon a “first come, first served” approach.  It is unfortunate that such a ruling has to be made, but it is necessary given there are only three courts and we do not wish to discourage adults from playing as this would not be in the best interests of adults or juniors.
