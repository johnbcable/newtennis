Daytime Tennis for Adults at Berkswell - All Welcome!

Day time coaching

for Ladies and Gentlemen

Join Ian at Berkswell Tennis Club on Tuesday, Thursday and Friday mornings 9.30 to 10.30am

Newcomers welcome!

 

The project to replace the courts at Hampton begins on Monday 2 February and luckily we have been able to secure an excellent alternative venue for our adult daytime coaching - Berkswell Tennis Club, Meeting House Lane, Balsall Common.  We are indeed extremely grateful to Berkswell for allowing us to use their courts.

 

The day time group coaching sessions are open to both ladies and gentlemen.  A wide range of abilities is catered for, so whatever your standard, you can join in. The sessions run during school term time.

 

So, here's your chance to get fit for 2009 and to practise your tennis skills in a friendly environment ready for the opening of our new top quality courts.
