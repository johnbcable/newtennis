Hampton-In-Arden Tennis Club

Spring 2007 - Adult Tennis Coaching

From mid-January 2007


Twiddling your thumbs on those dark nights or have some spare time during the day? Why not make sure your tennis skills don't go rusty over the winter period. Come along to one of these sessions and develop your technique ready for the 2007 season.

All of the following sessions are run by our LTA licensed tennis professional, Alan Cutts.

Thursday Mornings

All adults - all standards 9.30 - 10.30 am
Continue to develop your game alongside your friends, play matches with them and have our coach give you advice to improve your game. Includes coffee. Cost per person is £5 per session

Thursday Evenings: 8 - 9 pm

Intermediate advanced course 8 - 9 pm
A slightly more advanced programme to help you fine tune your game.

Each of the above programmes is offered as a 8-week course (a maximum of 8 places are available per course - book early to make sure of a place!) at a cost of £32.80 per person.

Friday (early afternoons)

All standards adult mix-in sessions 2.00 - 3.00 pm
All standards of play are welcome to come along and have our coach advise you on your game. Cost is £5 per session

To book your session(s), or for more information, help and advice, contact Alan on 07748 656002 or via alan@penntenn.net
