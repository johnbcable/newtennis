Announcing..the

C Cup & D Cup

In order to try to give every member a chance of playing on finals day (Sep 29) two new tournaments have been set up.

The rules and the draw are shown in the attached spreadsheet. All adult members in MAXI tennis categories C and D for  have been included in the competition.  (ie excluding category A and B players) The four semi finalists will be decided on who has the most points in each league by September 16th.

Please play as many of your games as you can - each game should take 35 minutes at the most.  If you don't wish to take part then don't worry - just don't play your matches.

We hope you enjoy the competition.

The Tournament committee

Defibrillator

The new defibrillator is now located in the clubhouse next to the front door. 

About 30 club members have been trained to use it including all bar staff so that there is always a high chance of someone trained being in the club at all times in the event of an emergency.

A list of those trained is kept behind the bar.
