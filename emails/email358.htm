Fixtures last for 90 minutes with teams consisting of 4 adults and 2 juniors. Each team member plays 2 doubles and 2 singles games. Games are won by the first player to win 10 points using tie break rules. As it is a team game, the highest team score wins the fixture - so every point counts.

The event is organised to ensure that any member can expect to play matches against other members of a similar standard. If you have not played Maxi-Tennis before and would like to know a bit more, please visit our web site.
