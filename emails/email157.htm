The Blues are Back

funkdory

with special guest

Dr Ika

BluesIsTheNews are back - with a bang!
Saturday 1st December, 2007
at the Clubhouse, Shadowbrook Lane
8 'til late

Cool jazz/ blues/funk band funkydory make a welcome return to Hampton In Arden to kick start a return to live blues music at the club. With a new album on the way, there'll be new material as well as established favourites to savour. The brain-child of guitarist Jules Fothergill and his wife Lorna Reilly (lead vocals), the 5-piece band are completed with lead guitarist Marc Le Guerranic, Hayden Doyle on drums and Ian Mauracio on bass. If you haven't got into this band yet, then you may be able to snap up a copy of their first album, "Still", at the gig.

Hailing from Georgia in the ex-USSR, Dr Ika became a favourite at record label Blues Matters before teaming up with our very own Peter Morris in a venture specialising in high quality amps and speaker cabinets. Sporting a vast range of guitar styles from delicate finger picking through to raucous, Jimi Hendrix covers, Dr Ika is a consumate guitarist who any budding player could learn a thing or two from!
