URGENT MESSAGE

Adult Tournament 2004

Finals Day (Saturday 10 July 2004) is now less than two weeks away and a number of matches remain to be played in a very short space of time!

If you are still in any of the tournaments, please can you give this your immediate focus.

I do not like having to disqualify people, but to be fair to all members, this may be necessary to ensure the tournament reaches a satisfactory conclusion.

I draw your attention to the following:

  1. There is no home league match on the Thursday 1 July; therefore all three courts are available for use.
     

  2. Attendance at the Sunday morning Club session has been poor, therefore on Sunday 4 July, the courts may be used for adult tournament matches.
     

  3. Please use the booking sheets that are on the tennis club notice board to reserve your court.
     

  4. A plate competition will still be held for early round losers in the singles competitions, but this will be held later in the year.

Please, please, please arrange your matches NOW!

Stephen Ray
(Chairman)
