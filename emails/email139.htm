Another Successful Play Tennis Day
5 May 2007
 
Our annual open day was held last Saturday and as usual, it was highly successful.  This year we had 108 people of various ages on court (but not all at the same time!).  For the first time, we also had hockey on offer thanks to the hockey section.
 
We are very grateful to everyone who came and to everyone who helped, both on court (Alan and his team) and off court.  The helpers are too numerous to mention individually and consisted of both adults and juniors.
