Wimbledon Ticket Draw

Friday 1st April, 2011

The draw for 36 Wimbledon ticket pairs will be made on Friday 1st April at the Clubhouse at 7.30pm.

The lsut of members to be included in the draw are:

Alastair Grant, Alex White, Alison Sabine, Brett McSkimming, Cameron Grant, Carol Betts, Carol Grant, Catherine Eley, Charlotte Brown, Charlotte Ray, Charlotte Sabine, David Betts, David Sabine, Deborah Naisbett, Edmund Roper, Elizabeth White, Emily Hodge, Emily White, Emma Roper, Emma Shalley, Geoff Redfern, Gill Lewis, Guy Hemington, Harry Goodworth, Harry Quinney, Helen Roper, Jack Naisbett, Jacob Morris, Jamie Turpin, Jane Durkin, Jane Hodge, Jane Imlah, Jenny Roberts, Jess Betts, Jo Ray, Joshua Morris, Julian Lee, Julie Williamson, Kat Gaspar, Lewis White, Liam Haggerty, Malcolm Brumwell, Margaret Elsy, Marianna Lee, Mark Walker, Matt Hunt, Mel Warner, Mike Durkin, Miles Goodworth, Oliver Ray, Ollie White, Paul Roper, Philip Turpin, Ray Barnett, Ro Eccleston, Ruth Fennell, Sarah Trainer, Sarah White, Savio Gaspar, Simon Jeavons, Simon Turpin, Sophie Morris, Stephen Morris, Steve Ray, Steve White, Sue Charlton, Tom Betts, Will Knight, Yvonne Jones

If you are not in the list of members above and you would like to enter the draw please reply to this email by Thursday March 31st. You must be a tennis member, at least 10 years old on June 20th and a BTM member. Draw winners will receive a pair of tickets and are free to choose any guest to take with them, although under 16's must be accompanied by an adult. Tickets cost from £64 and £220 a pair.

Please note that under LTA rules, tickets are not transferable so if you cannot go you should let us know quickly so that we can reallocate the tickets to a member on the reserve list. If you do not follow this rule you run the risk of jeopardising the club's future ticket allocation.

Thank you for your assistance.
