Tennis, Squash and Racket Ball Open Day

Saturday 14 May, from 1pm

Now you play tennis, perhaps you would like to try squash or racket ball? Or maybe you have friends or colleagues who would like to play tennis, squash or racket ball? If so, please join us next Saturday afternoon.

All ages and abilities are warmly welcomed. The activities are offered free of charge. Please wear appropriate footwear.

Tennis

  • 1pm - 2pm Juniors
  • 2pm - 3pm Adults

Squash/Racketball

  • 1pm - 2pm Juniors
  • 2pm - 3pm Adults

For more information, please contact Ian Poole on 07968 451956.

Hope to see you on Saturday!
