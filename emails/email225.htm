Lighting on Court 3 

 We have had to take down the lighting pole nearest the Scout and Guide Hut today for safety reasons as it was in danger of falling over.

We were hoping that it would last until the new courts are constructed but unfortunately it has not lasted long enough.  This means that the end court is not properly lit at the moment.

We are looking at suitable alternatives to tide us over until we build the new courts.  In the meantime thank you for your understanding and we will let you know as soon as we have sorted out the interim arrangement.
