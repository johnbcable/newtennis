REMINDER: Red Mini Tennis Day

Including Tots Tennis

1pm Sunday 13 July, 2008


All red mini-tennis players and tots  are invited to attend our special event.

Tots will take part in a session that will show what they have been learning. Starting sport at such a young age is a huge advantage, so come along and see what they get up to.

For our red mini-tennis players there will be fun and games plus a tournament for the older, more experienced players.

We hope to have as many spectators as possible, so please come and support the juniors at this event which has been organised specially for them.

Bad weather on tournament day?

We do our very best to run scheduled tournaments despite the weather but as always our primary concern is safety for everyone using the courts. So, if it is raining heavily on the day of the tournament, the event will be cancelled or postponed. If the weather is uncertain, please phone Ian (07968 451956) to check if it is still going to take place.
