2005 Tournament Draw


The tournament draw for 2005 has now been made and the completed draw has been posted on the club notice board.

A copy of the draw is attached to this email for your reference (in Adobe Acrobat (.PDF) format). You can also visit the web site and download a copy in Microsoft Word format if this of more use to you

Please arrange and play all your matches promptly - we don't want an unseemly last-minute scramble for courts again this year! Don't forget: the only way people know you have reserved a court/time for your match is if you complete the court booking sheets held by the noticeboard in the club house

Best of luck to everyone in this competition and fingers-crossed for good weather over the whole period between now and finals day which is on

Saturday 2 July, 2005
