Tournament Draw 2006
 

Please find attached the 2006 tournament draw and related guidelines/instructions. When arranging your matches please note:

  1. When junior coaching tales place on a Saturday morning, for the majority of the time only 2 of the 3 available courts are used - please see the court booking sheets on the tennis club notice board for full details
     
  2. During the school half-term week (week commencing Monday 29th May) there are no adult league matches and there is no junior coaching on Friday 2nd June or Saturday 3rd June. Hence, this is a great week to get matches played if you (or your opponents) are not on holiday!

Have a great time playing in the tournament and please make sure that you play your matches promptly to allow subsequent rounds the necessary time to make their arrangements
