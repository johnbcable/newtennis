Hampton-In-Arden Sports Club

CLUB NOTICE

Car Park Improvements

Temporary closure of tennis courts 
 Friday 14th May – Sunday 16th May Inclusive

 

 

The Parish Council have obtained planning permission to increase the car park area.

 

Contractors are removing the spoil to the new football pitches on the above dates and will require access over the area between the tennis courts and the clubhouse.

 

For Health & Safety reasons the following will apply:

  • Pedestrian access will be available to the clubhouse.
  • Car park will be closed to traffic – please use the station car park.
  • The tennis courts, recreation ground and scouts and guides building will not be available for use.

The schedule of work is subject to weather conditions and a final decision will be made on Wednesday 12th May.  Notice of the decision will be posted on the tennis club web site home page by 8pm on Thursday 13th May.

 

Thank you for your co-operation.

 

 

John Eccleston

Chairman

NOTICE

Construction of All Weather Pitch on Recreation Ground 
 Starting Monday 17th May

 

Contractors are scheduled to start work on Monday 17th May.  Please note the following:

  • Contractors will access the recreation ground via the double gates close to the Spinney on Shadowbrook Lane.
  • A temporary roadway is to be laid, adjacent to the Spinney, to the site area.
  • The roadway and site area will be enclosed by fencing.
  • The project is due to complete early September subject to weather conditions.
