Spring 2015 Junior Coaching Programme

Monday 5th January to Saturday 28th March
(excluding half term, week beginning 16th February).

We are now making plans for the spring 2015 coaching programme. If you are not planning to carry on with lessons or if you need to change group or time, please speak to your current coach before the end of term.

We have set out below the fees for next term and attached a booking form.

The courses start on Monday 5th January and run for 11 weeks. The coaching fees are as follows:

1-hour lessons £67.00
45-minute lessons £55.00
30-minute lessons £43.00

An early payment discount of £5 may be applied to the above fees if paid by 31st December. Fees that remain outstanding by the third week of term will incur a late payment penalty of £5
