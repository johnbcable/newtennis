New for 2013 - Hampton Tennis Club Blog!

Please visit our new tennis club blog and sign up to receive up to date information about the junior programme at Hampton. We plan to use the blog to keep you informed of important information about the junior programme such as amendments, cancellations and venue changes (in bad weather). Once you have signed up, you will receive email notification of any new posts on the blog. As we do not wish to clog up your in-box, posts will be restricted to important information only (for tennis club updates, results and gossip please see our Facebook page or follow us on Twitter)

Summer Term Course Fees

As you may have noticed, we have record numbers of children on the mini-tennis programme and the courts are full every evening after school as we approach the busy tennis season. Therefore, to guarantee your child's place in their current lesson we will need to receive your payment for the summer term by the last lesson of this term, the 23rd March 2013. Please note that we cannot guarantee your child's place in their current lesson if your fees are not received by this date. If your child is due to move up into a new class, a coach will contact you before the end of term to find a convenient class for you. Again, we will need to receive your fees by the end of this term to secure your child's place in the new class.
