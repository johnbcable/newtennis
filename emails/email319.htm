No coaching this Saturday 1 May and Monday 3 May due to:

The Sports Club Open Day and the Bank Holiday

 

 

 

 

The normal group coaching sessions will NOT take place this weekend due to the Sports Club Open Day and the Bank Holiday. Lessons resume on Tuesday 4 May.

 

 

However, you are warmly invited to our Open Day to try out a number of sports, without charge.  Also we will be officially opening the new Euro-clay tennis courts and the mini red court. There will be other attractions too, including boules and face painting.  Refreshments will be available.

 

Both members and non-members are welcome. 

 

Here's the programme of events:

 

Tennis

10.00 - 10.40  Juniors - senior school age 11+

11.00 - 11.40  Orange & Green mini juniors - ages 8 to 10

12.00 - 12.40  Tots & Red mini juniors - ages 7 & under, Plus mums & dads

 2.00 - 3.00  Adult tennis coaching - all standards

 3.00 onwards - Mix-in

 

Hockey

10.15 - 10.45 Juniors aged 7 to 11

10.45 - 11.15 Juniors - senior school age 11+

 

Squash/racket ball

11.40 - 12.10 Mini-squash for juniors aged 8 to 12

12.40 -  1.00 Mini-squash for juniors aged 5 to 7

 3.00 - 4.30 Adult squash & racket ball taster session

 

Fitness sessions (provided by The Hampton Gym)

9.45 - 10.00 Morning warm-up.  Other sessions during the day

9.45 onwards  Complimentary sports injury clinic

 

~Official Opening of the New Tennis Courts~

1.00 - 2.00 Official Opening including exhibition tennis by juniors & adults

 

We do hope you can join us for this special event!
