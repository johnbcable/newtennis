Update

  

As some of you will be aware the service lines on all four courts were originally located 5cm away from where they should have been.  Unfortunately the instructions to correct this error last week were not followed correctly, so the process will have to be repeated this coming week over half term.

 

Unfortunately this means that the courts will be out of action and locked from 12.30pm on Tuesday May 26th to (weather permitting) till 6pm on Friday May 29th.  Specifically therefore:

  • Ian Poole's coaching camp on Tuesday May 26th will run as advertised.
  • Social Tennis on Tuesday May 26th is cancelled.
  • Social Tennis on Friday May 29th will run as normal.

 

We have been asked by the LTA Consultant, who has been appointed by the club as Project Manager, to let you know that the removal and relaying of a one metre wide strip of carpet is perfectly acceptable.

 

We apologise for any inconvenience but hope that you understand our desire to ensure that the courts are laid out correctly.

 

Thank you.
