A Tennis Weekend - 4th and 5th July, 2008

This weekend is action-packed with tennis events.

On Saturday there is our annual club adult and junior finals day starting with the Under 16 Boys Tournament at 11.30am. Please come and support the players. There is also the Wimbledon Ladies singles final being screened from 2pm.

And please don't forget 14-year old Brit Laura Robson (unseeded) who is playing in the Wimbledon girls junior singles final against the number 3 seed Noppawan Lertcheewakarn from Thailand. She is the second match on court one and court one matches start at approx. 2.00pm

On Sunday we will be screening the Wimbledon Mens singles final. The start time has not been announced on the Wimbledon website as yet, but it is likely to be between 1pm and 2pm. If you would like to join in some social tennis too PLEASE BRING YOUR RACKET. Everyone is welcome.
