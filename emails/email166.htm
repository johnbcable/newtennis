Message from the LTA

Please sign up to British Tennis before Dec 31

Dear Member

I am writing to inform you of a unique opportunity the Lawn Tennis Association (LTA) is providing to members of LTA affiliated clubs.

The LTA is offering you FREE 2008 membership to British Tennis, its new membership programme.

To access this FREE 2008 membership, please click here:

http://www.lta.org.uk/Membership/MembershipReview

British Tennis membership includes a broad range of valuable benefits such as a player rating, membership card, advice from professional coaches and top players, an e-newsletter, special members-only website content, information on competitions and events, and you should have privileged entry to our club’s Wimbledon ticket ballot. 

This membership and benefits are only free to members of affiliated clubs such as ours.

Joining British Tennis means becoming part of the British Tennis Family of 1 million-plus members. It means building a strong foundation for a winning British game. It means being recognised for your contribution to tennis. It means supporting tennis from the grass roots to Centre Court.

We would encourage you to join now to become a part of the future of British Tennis.

Thank you

Chris Barnes (text as drafted by the LTA)
