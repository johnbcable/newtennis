Rules

Phone numbers are accessible on the tennis web site.  If you don't have a password please apply to John Cable at support@hamptontennis.org.uk

  1. Matches should last for 60 to 75 minutes allowing for a 10 minute knock up.  They are played as five championship tie break "sets" under normal championship tie break rules except that there is no need for a lead of 2 points - so 10-9 is a winning score.

  2. The score starts at 0,0 in "set" 1.  In "set" 2 the score starts at +1,-1 in favour of the pair who lost the first "set".  In set 3 the score starts at +2,-2 if one pair wins the first two "sets" or alternatively reverts back to 0,0 if the first two "sets" are shared.   In "set" 4 the score starts at +3,-3 if one pair has won the first three sets or at +1,-1 in favour of the team which is down by 2 "sets" to 1.  In "set" 5 the score starts +4,-4 if one pair has lost all four "sets", or +2,-2 if the score in "sets" is 3 to1 or 0,0 if the score in "sets" is 2-2.

  3. The winning pair is the pair winning most "sets"
