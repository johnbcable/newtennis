Please could the winners send a cheque payable to Hampton Sports Club to Chester at 68 Meriden Road, Hampton-in-Arden B92 0BT. Following receipt of the cheque your tickets will be sent to you. If you do not wish to take up your ticket allocation please could you either email me at secretary@hamptontennis.org.uk or call me on . In the event that someone cannot take up their ticket allocation I will organise another draw to allocate the tickets. 

Please could the winners note the following LTA rules which we have been asked to draw to your attention: 

  • Tickets must NOT be resold above face value under any circumstances.

  • Tickets must only be issued to club members for their own and their guests use. 

  • Club Secretaries are obliged to make a return of the names of members to whom tickets have been allocated and to sign an undertaking that those members will abide by the conditions laid down by the All England Club. 
Thank you.
