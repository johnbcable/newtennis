Calling all Captains - The League Season Approaches!

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Dear ZZforenameZZ,

The tennis league season starts for teams in the Coventry and District Lawn Tennis League from Monday 26 April 2010. I know you are all busy working away getting things ready, but I would like to draw your attention to one or two things:

  1. As you may have noticed/worked out, the fixture booklet as sent out by the league was incorrectly set out for the Hampton teams - it had us playing 4 teams at home on the same Monday nights! This has been resolved now by swapping round two of our teams positions in the division lists with other teams.

    The Ladies second team swap with Kenilworth 4 Ladies - this makes Hampton team 8 in the Ladies 5the Division and Kenilworth 4 Ladies as team 1.

    The Mens 3rd team also swap in their (5the) Division with Beechwood 5. This makes Hampton team 8 in the Mens 5the Division and Beechwood 5 as team 1.

    Both of the above Hampton teams retain their Monday night home fixture night but will play on the different Monday night to the Mens 1st and 4th teams.

    The web site has all the updated fixtures.
  2. There appears to have been no change in the rules this year.
  3. The Coventry League web site has moved this year to
    http://www.coventrytennisleague.co.uk. The club web site has updated its links accordingly.
  4. There have been some changes in contact details for some of your opposing clubs and one club has moved its ground (Caludon Castle) although this doesn't affect our teams this year. The web site has updated details.

 

This season it would be really great if you all did send a brief email for each match telling the story for the rest of the members - win or lose. It helps every one keep up-to-date and supportive.

You can also put your results in online directly to the club web site if you wish. Let me know if this is something you'd like to do and I'll send a brief note out to you letting you know how to do it.

Good luck to you all in the season ahead!
