Wimbledon Tickets
for Summer 2011

If you would like to enter the draw please email wimbledontickets@hamptontennis.org.uk by Thursday March 31st. You must be a tennis member, at least 10 years old on June 20th and a BTM member. Draw winners will receive a pair of tickets and are free to choose any guest to take with them, although under 16's must be accompanied by an adult. Tickets cost from £64 and £220 a pair. Please note that under LTA rules, tickets are not transferable so if you cannot go you should let us know quickly so that we can reallocate the tickets to a member on the reserve list. Please note that if you do not follow this rule you risk jeopardising the club's future ticket allocation. Under no circumstances should you sell your tickets.

The draw is earlier than normal this year as the process is driven by LTA requirements for ticket holder names to be sent to the LTA in early May. The draw will be made at the clubhouse on Friday April 1st at 7.30pm and the results distributed by email. If you are successful please send a cheque payable to Hampton-in-Arden Sports Club to Wayman Morris who lives behind the Sports Club in the house with a steep in and out drive at Shadowbrook, Shadowbrook Lane, Hampton-in-Arden, Solihull B92 0DQ and make arrangements to collect the tickets by Saturday April 16th. Wayman can be contacted on 01675 442071 or at waymanmorris@mail.com. If you do not contact Wayman we will assume that you do not wish to buy the tickets.

All tickets not taken up will be offered by email to reserves on Tuesday April 19th and allocated on the basis of the earliest telephone call received by Wayman. If you do not wish to take up your allocation, please let Wayman know as soon as possible to help us to find homes quickly for all tickets allocated to the club.
