Hampton-In-Arden Tennis Club is now an official registered RAW tennis centre. Our coach, Alan Cutts, will be featuring RAW tennis practices in his coaching for 10-18 year olds from now on.

So, What is in RAW Tennis?

RAW tennis features 4 main elements:

  • FREESTYLE - skills, flicks and tricks with a tennis ball

  • ENERGY - fitness activities related to tennis that can be used in tennis lessons, in the park or in the garden

  • PB's - an opportunity for everyone to get recognised for their individual achievements. Players can measure their own progress on-court through Personal Best's. And finally,

  • SLAMS - for those that want to, they can participate in quick-fire team competitions involving singles and doubles matches held at our club or others who also support RAW tennis.
