Thank you very much to those people who have already paid the coaching fees for next term.  However, for those who haven't, here's a quick reminder to let you know that to qualify for prompt payment discount, payment must be made to Ian by this coming Saturday at the latest.

If you do not wish to rejoin the programme, please let Ian know by the end of this week, so that he can plan his groups accordingly. Ian can be contacted on 07968 451956

A schedule and a booking form for the Spring 2010 junior coaching programme are attached.  The costs and also discounts and surcharges are given below. 

All juniors aged 7 or over on 1 May 2009 must be members of the tennis club.  However, attendees who are new to the courses can play for half a term without being members whilst they decide whether tennis at Hampton is for them.  For more information on joining the tennis club, please click here.

Juniors aged under 7 on 1 May 2009 have to be on the club database. There is no charge for this.
