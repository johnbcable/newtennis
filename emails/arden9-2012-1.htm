K & D ASICS Arden 9-Mile Road Race

www.arden9.org.uk

Sunday 24 June 2012 - 10.30am

Hampton-in-Arden Sports Club B92 0DQ

Thank you very much for taking part in previous Arden 9 mile road races. We are both delighted and excited to announce that for the first time in 2012 the ASICS Arden 9 mile road race will be hosted by Knowle & Dorridge Running Club and has also been accepted as part of the 2012 Warwickshire Road Race League.

Online entries only please through Runners World: http://www.runnersworld.co.uk/kdasicsarden9

Entry fee £10 (for UK Athletics affiliated club members) or £12 otherwise.
No credit card fees.
www.arden9.org.uk

The entry fee is £12 with a £2 discount if you are a UK Athletics affiliated club member

As in previous years the race is intended for both regular runners and novices. It has been approved by the Midland Counties Athletic Association as a race to be held under the current laws of competition of the Amateur Athletic Association of England and in accordance with the bye-laws of the Midland Counties Athletic Association. The course has been measured as nine miles by the UK Athletics Course Measurement Scheme.
