Autumn 2013 Junior Coaching Programme

Tuesday 3 September to Saturday 14 December 2013

(excluding half term, week beginning 28th October)

We've had a fantastic summer term including some good weather and we are already planning ahead for the Autumn term. As the mini tennis programme is running close to capacity, we are asking everyone to pay their coaching fees on or before the last lesson of this term, Saturday 20th July in order to guarantee their place in the same lesson next term.

We have set out below the fees for next term and attached a booking form.

The courses start on Tuesday 3rd September and run for 14 weeks. The coaching fees are as follows:

1-hour lessons £82.00
45-minute lessons £67.00
30-minute lessons £52.00

An early payment discount of £5 may be applied to the above fees if paid by 20th July. Fees that remain outstanding by the third week of term will incur a late payment penalty of £5
