Spring 2007 - MAXI-Tennis Update


Once again, well done Captains! Special thanks to Carol Grant for emailing the typed results - much appreciated!

This weeks contests saw the England squad fall to their first defeat against a bubbling-under French side; Ireland winning against Scotland to go 4th (more later) and Italy once again triumphing (just about) against last years champions, Wales.

This leaves the league table in quite an interesting position - with any upsets next week potentially causing a 5-way choice for finalists! To help with clarifying the position if that happens, this is how the order in the league table is decided: the first thing taken into consideration is the league points obtained by each team (2 for a win; 1 for a draw; 0 for a loss). In the event of a tie with league points, then the Total Tennis Games for each team are taken into consideration. If there is still a tie after these two, then the Total Tennis Points is called into play to help decide who is placed higher in the league. If all of those are tied .............. we'll get back to you!

Just a reminder, all the results (incl. details of individual fixture and game scores) are up on the web site for everyone to see, even if they are not going to be involved in the tournament this time around.
