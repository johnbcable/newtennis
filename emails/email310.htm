Easter 2010 Tennis and Multi-Sport Camps

To be held at Hampton-In-Arden Sports Club

Under 8's Camps,  9am - 12 noon,  Friday 9th and Friday 16th April

Over 8's Camps (orange, green and full ball), 9am - 12 noon, Tuesday 6th and Tuesday 13th April

Cost is £8.50 per camp    (£9.50 for non-members)

To be held at Lady Katherine Leveson Primary School

Juniors of all ages,  9am - 3.30pm,  Monday to Friday, 12th to 16th April

Cost is £20 per day, £75 per week.    (£18 per day if booking more than one child)

 

We have Easter camps for juniors of all ages, so come along, have fun and get some exercise! Open to both members and non-members so please feel free to bring a friend.
