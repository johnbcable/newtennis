FURTHER AMENDMENT: 2008 Annual Mini-Tennis Tournaments


Red Mini Tennis Tournament - Change of Date

Due to clashes with various other events, it has been decided to change the date of the Red Mini Tennis tournament from this Saturday, 7 June from 2pm to 

1pm, Sunday 13 July.


The event will include both fun and games plus a tournament for the older, more advanced players.

We apologise for any inconvenience caused, but hopefully more children will be able to take part as a result of the change.

Please note that the Mini Tennis Tournament as scheduled for Sunday 8 June from 1pm will go ahead as planned.
