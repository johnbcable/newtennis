Hampton-In-Arden Tennis Club

Temporary Junior Coaching Changes

 

 

New Tennis Courts!  Temporary Changes, from Friday 6 Feb

 

Next Monday, work will begin on replacing the tennis courts with four new ones plus a Kidzone.  The project is estimated to take 14 weeks with the new courts being available sometime  in early May. So, in the mean time there will be some changes to the Friday night junior coaching. We apologise for this disruption and thank you for your understanding.

 

Sessions will take place on the astroturf pitch and will involve a variety of physical activities.  As a gesture of goodwill to compensate for the disruption, the sessions will last for an hour and a half rather than just an hour.  The 6.00pm and 7.00pm groups will now run from 6.00 to 7.30 pm.  Astroboots are preferable, but not essential.

 

In addition we have organised some activities which will take place on Saturdays and are being offered complimentary. The children are invited to attend a gym session, supervised by a qualified trainer at The Hamptons on Old Station Road from 10.30 to 11.30.  Also on offer is racket ball from 11.30 to 12.30.  Alternatively, there are some supervised tennis sessions on Saturdays.  They will take place from 10.30am to 12.30pm on the courts at Eversfield School, Warwick Road on 7 and 14 Feb.  Additional sessions will be organised if there is sufficient demand.
