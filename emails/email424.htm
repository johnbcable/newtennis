Tournament Draw 2012 - UPDATED

The [UPDATED] 2012 tournament draw for all competitions is attached. It is also on display on the tennis club noticeboard and has been posted on the web site from Tuesday this week [21 May].

Deadlines - To remind all competitors, the deadlines for playing matches are as follows:

  • Last 16 games - Sunday May 27th
  • Quarter Finals - Sunday June 10th
  • Semi Finals - Sunday June 24th
  • Finals day is Saturday July 7th

Please note that there will be limited court space available in the week leading up to finals day so please can you try your best not to delay the competition?

Booking a court - You can check court availability and book courts by going to www.hamptontennis.org.uk, clicking on the "Book a court" link and then logon with your User ID and PIN. If you need help or you have forgotten your User ID or PIN please email support@hamptontennis.org.uk.

Regards,Geoff Redfern,Tournament Committee Chairman
