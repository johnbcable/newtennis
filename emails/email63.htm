Kenilworth Tennis Club
Festival of Fun Doubles for all Junior Members


Kenilworth Tennis Club are running a two-day junior doubles competition on the following two dates:

Wednesday 27 July, 2005 (Inter Club Doubles), and


Friday 29 July, 2005 (Open Junior American Doubles)

This event is to be held at their club and is open to all doubles pairs in any category. The entrance fee is £3.50/person per event. Completed application forms (copy in Adobe Acrobat format attached to this email - the web site also contains copies in Acrobat and Microsoft Word format for you to download) should be submitted by Monday 25 July and Wednesday 27 July (respectively) to:

Mrs A Holmes,
9 Woodland Road,
Kenilworth,
Warwickshire,
CV8 2FJ

The application form contains more points of contact with the organisers if you need to speak with them about detailed arrangements. This sort of one-day tournament is ideal for you to increase your match play skills outside the more formal confines of league tennis - and its cheap AND the weather will be great!
