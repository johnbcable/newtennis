2010 Annual Junior Club Tournaments

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

U12 and U16 tournaments take place this coming weekend

 

 

 

Our annual U12 and U16 tournaments are taking place next weekend. If you haven't already entered, but would like to, please fill in the form below and give it to Ian together with the entry fee. This is a great opportunity to put into practice what you have been learning during your coaching sessions, so we hope that you will enter.

 


Tournament Age Time and Date
U12 Boys Under 12 on 1 Jan 2010 3pm to 6pm, Sun 20 Jun
U12 Girls Under 12 on 1 Jan 2010 3pm to 6pm, Sun 20 Jun
U16 Boys Under 16 on 1 Jan 2010 1pm to 3pm, Sat 19 Jun
U16 Girls Under 16 on 1 Jan 2010 3pm to 6pm, Sat 19 Jun

 

There will be medals for winners and runners-up. The finals will be played on the Senior and Junior Club Finals Day on Saturday 3 July, 2010. Finalists must be available for this date, please.

 

 

 

Please note: The above competitions are open to all junior members except those with a LTA rating of 9.2 or lower. However, the U16 Boys and U16 Girls competitions are open to any members of that age group.


Please don't forget participants who are aged 7 or older as at 1 April 2010 must be MEMBERS of the club. If you wish to know more, contact Chester Barnes on , go onto our website www.hamptontennis.org.uk, or pick up a membership application form from the tennis notice board in the club house.

 

Bad weather on tournament day?

If the weather is uncertain, please phone Ian (07968 451956) to check if it is still going to take place.
