Jubilee Tennis for All!

From 11am, Monday 4 June 2012

Please bring your family and friends to our Jubilee tennis event! There will be a session for all standards and ages followed by tennis for adults and juniors aged 7 and over:

  • 11am - 1pm: Daytime: All ages

  • 1pm onwards: Daytime: Free play tennis session for adults and juniors aged 7 or over.

Please remember to wear suitable footwear - non-ridged trainers or tennis shoes. And of course, red, white and blue sports wear if you have it!

Hope to see you on the 4th.
