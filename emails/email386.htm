REMINDER: Social Tennis Tournament

Saturday 17 September,
from 2.00pm until (approx) 4.30pm

Please see the attached flyer advertising our mixed-doubles, round robin social tennis tournament.

Entrance fee is £3 per person ... and this gets you a glass of bubbly between rounds and a finale of afternoon tea served on completion of the final rubbers!

As this a round-robin tournament, you do not have to enter as doubles pairs because your partner will change after each round.

Players must be ready to start playing at 2.00pm and so should plan to arrive a little earlier to get warmed up

Response has been good - the ladies deserve a "really well done" badge as there have been 13 requests to play. However we are still short of 2 men. Currently confirmed entrants are shown below. Apologies to those ladies that have missed out this time - a second day can be planned if we can rouse enough fellas!
