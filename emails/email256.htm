Semi-Final Tustle

A phone-in report tonight at approx. 9,20 pm has confirmed that our Mini-Red tennis team succeeded in getting to the semi-finals of the County Cup where they were very unfortunate in losing to Solihull Arden by a single point!
Well done to everyone involved!!
