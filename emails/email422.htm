Tournament Draw 2012

The 2012 tournament draw for all competitions except the mens doubles and the mixed doubles plate is attached. It is also on display on the tennis club noticeboard and will be posted on the web site in the early part of this week.

Deadlines - The deadlines for playing matches are as follows:

  • Last 16 games - Sunday May 27th
  • Quarter Finals - Sunday June 10th
  • Semi Finals - Sunday June 24th
  • Finals day is Saturday July 7th

Please note that there will be limited court space available in the week leading up to finals day so please can you try your best not to delay the competition?

Late entries - If you would like to enter any of the tournaments and you are not yet in the draw please can you reply to this email by Wednesday May 16th latest and we will do our best to include you.

Mens Doubles - The draw will be released by next Sunday.

Mixed Doubles Plate - At the moment we have 21 men and 12 ladies signed up. Please could we have some more volunteers to play other than the following 12 ladies? Jayne Barnes, Carol Betts, Catherine Eley, Jane Durkin, Katerina Gaspar, Yvonne Gregory, Deborah Jones, Sally Leach, Pauline Morris, Emma Shalley, Karen Vithanage, Sandy Wasse.

Booking a court - You can check court availability and book courts by going to www.hamptontennis.org.uk, clicking on the "Book a court" link and then logon with your User ID and PIN. If you need help or you have forgotten your User ID or PIN please email support@hamptontennis.org.uk.

Regards,Geoff Redfern,Tournament Committee Chairman
