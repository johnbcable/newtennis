Using the courts and floodlights

Booking courts

 You may use the booking sheets on the notice board to book a court.  Maximum booking period per member per day is 2 hours.

 

Adults take priority in the week after 6pm.  Children take priority on the mini tennis court at all times.

 

 

Combination lock

The code for the combination locks on the external gates is 4211.  Please lock all three gates if you are last to leave.  Please do not reveal this code to non-members. 

 

Shoes on court 

  Sadly there have been a few instances of wearing the wrong shoes on court.  We repeat that it is VERY IMPORTANT that you (or the children you are responsible for) wear tennis shoes or non marking trainers to play.  Definitely no running shoes, studs, blades, grooves or defined heels.  Incorrect footwear WILL significantly damage the courts. 

 

Brushing after use

  When you finish play please take your turn to brush the courts so that they are ready for the next players.

Floodlights

 

(1) Floodlight tokens can be bought at the bar for £2 each.

 

(2) To turn on the lights feed a token into the meter in the clubhouse.  Courts 1 to 3 are the three court block from left to right and court 4 is the single court.

 

(3) Each token lights one court for 30 minutes.

 

(4) You will get 5 minutes warning from the coloured lights on the posts before the lights turn off.

 

(5) Please note that the floodlights automatically turn off at 10pm.
