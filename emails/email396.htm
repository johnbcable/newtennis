Spring 2012 Junior Coaching Programme

Wednesday 4 January to Saturday 7 April 2012

(excluding half term, week beginning 13 February)

Many thanks to our juniors, parents and carers for your support during 2011. We hope you will continue to enjoy tennis at Hampton during 2012.

The spring term will be starting in just a few weeks and a coaching schedule and booking form are attached. The various coaching fees, including the discounted and surcharged rates, are given below.

The courses start on Wednesday 4 January. The Wednesday, Thursday, Friday and Saturday lessons run for 13 weeks whilst the Monday and Tuesday lessons run for 12 weeks. The costs are as follows:

All juniors aged 7 or over on 1 April 2011 must be members of the tennis club. However, attendees who are new to the courses can play for half a term without being members whilst they decide whether tennis at Hampton is for them. For more information on joining the tennis club, please visit our web site

Juniors aged under 7 on 1 April 2011 have to be registered on the club database. There is no charge for this.
