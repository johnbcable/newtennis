JUNIOR DOUBLES SUMMER COMPETITION 2006 !

This summer holidays event is open to all junior members who are capable of full-court rallying.

Each junior pair will be put into a group. Any pairings are allowed regardless of age/gender. If you cant find a partner, we will find one for you. Each pair then plays every other pair in their group

You must arrange your matches yourself. These can be played at any time the courts are free and not booked. A court session has been booked most Wednesday afternoons during the summer holidays from 2 till 5 pm

If you would like to take part, please print off and complete the attached entry form and return by Saturday 24 June to Jayne Barnes or Alan Cutts (address on the form)

Any questions, please speak to Alan or Jayne ()
