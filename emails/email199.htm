Warwickshire LTA (Lawn Tennis Association) produce a newsletter twice a year called "On the Ball". This is now available in electronic format and a copy is attached for your information.


In particular (in this issue), you may be interested in the following:

Page 1: Information about Hampton winning Warwickshire Tennis Club of the Year.
Pages 10 - 16: Details of both junior and senior tournaments around Warwickshire.
Page 17: Information about the DFS Women's International Tournament to be held at Edgbaston Priory Club
Happy reading!.
