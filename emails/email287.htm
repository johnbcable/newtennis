Coventry League promotions

 

Congratulations to the Mens and Ladies 2nd teams who have both been promoted this summer.

For the Mens 2nd team it was a third consecutive promotion.  Next season they will play in division 2.

For the Ladies 2nd team it was a promotion to take them back into division 5 at the first attempt.

Commiserations to the Mens 1st team who were relegated (just) from the premier league on "goal" difference.  Tough luck in a tough league.
