Results of Wimbledon Ticket Draw 2010

 

 

 

 

 

 

The results of the draw conducted at the Sports Club on Friday 21st May are shown below. If you wish to take up the ticket allocation please send a cheque made out to Hampton-in-Arden Sports Club to Chris Barnes at 68 Meriden Road, Hampton-in-Arden, Solihull B92 0BT by Friday 4th June. If no cheque is received by Friday 4th June we will assume that you do not wish to take up your ticket allocation and the unclaimed tickets will be offered to members on the reserve list. Please note that there will be no reminder to pay if we do not receive your cheque. If you cannot go please could you reply to this email as soon as possible to help us?

 

The named person in draw may nominate a guest of their own choosing who does not have to be a member of the tennis club, provided that if the named person is under 16 he or she must be accompanied by an adult guest.

 

Please note that under LTA rules the tickets are NOT transferable. This means that if the named member cannot go, the ticket allocation must be returned to the club to be offered to a member drawn on the reserve list in the sequence shown below. Please note that if you do not follow this rule you run the risk of jeopardising the club's future ticket allocation.

 

Thank you for your assistance.
