Junior "Road To Wimbledon" Tournament


Open to junior members born 1994 or later

From 2.00 pm
Sunday 18 May 2008

Entry fee £3 per person

Fully paid up members ONLY, please.

Please contact Ian (Poole - head coach) by Wednesday 14 May if you are interested. Tel 07968 451956.

Good luck to all entrants!

Spectators are very encouraged to come and support our juniors.

Look forward to seeing you!
