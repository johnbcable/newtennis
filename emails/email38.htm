Leaving Presentation for Caroline

Friday 22 October 2004, 7pm

You may be aware that our coach, Caroline, is moving to the Lake District after half term.  Caroline has been a great asset to our club over the few months she has been with us and she will be greatly missed by the children and other players who have come into contact with her.

To wish Caroline well and to say thank you for all her hard work, there will be a brief presentation at the club this Friday at 7pm. All are welcome to attend.
