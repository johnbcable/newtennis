Welcome to Hampton-in-Arden Tennis Club!

Thank you for joining Hampton-in-Arden Tennis Club. We hope that you really enjoy being a member.

To help to get you started, here are the answers to the more common questions asked:

  • How do I get into the clubhouse? If you are aged 18 or over you are entitled to a members key card which can also be used to buy drinks at the bar. This will be available for you to collect within a week from the bar whenever it is open. If you are aged 14 to 17 or if you are the parent of a child who is a tennis member you may ask at the bar on Tuesday evenings for a door access fob.

  • Where do I find the telephone numbers of other members? A list of members (16 or over) can be found in the password-protected members section of www.hamptontennis.org.uk. Your ID and password are the same as those you use to book courts.

  • What is the code for the court padlock? 3030

  • Any other questions? You can find the answers to any other questions you may have in our beginning of season newsletter, the Court Circular, which you can find by clicking on this link: Current Court Circular
