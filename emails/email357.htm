Spring 2011 Junior Coaching Programme

Monday 3 January to Saturday 9 April 2011

(excluding half term, week beginning 21 February)

The Spring term has now started. Thank you to those parents who have already paid their coaching fees. For those who haven't yet paid, may we politely remind you that the fees are now due and there is a surcharge for fees outstanding after the third lesson of the term (please see below).

We have arranged for indoor facilities at the Scout and Guide Hut and at Fentham Hall for our younger players for the first half term. The groups involved are given below. We are asking parents to contribute £1 per session towards the cost we are incurring to hire these facilities. Obviously, this is a little extra expense, but we hope you agree it is worth it to keep the younger children warm as well as fit and active.

The tennis courses recommences on Monday 3 January run for 13 weeks. The costs are as follows:

1-hour lessons £65
1-hour lessons (indoors)* £71
45-minute lessons £52
45-minute lessons (indoors)* £58
30-minute lessons £39

*An extra £6 is added as a contribution towards indoor facilities for the following red groups:

  • Monday 4-5 pm George Fentham Hall
  • Tuesday 3.45-4.30 pm George Fentham Hall
  • Wednesday 3.45-4.30 pm Scout and Guide Hut
  • Thursday 4-4.45 pm Scout and Guide Hut

Chasing payment is time consuming and tedious. There will be a surcharge for late payments not received by the 3rd lesson of term.

    Late Payment
(If you pay after the third 2011 lesson)
1-hour lessons   £70
1-hour lessons (indoors)*   £76
45-minute lessons   £56
45-minute lessons (indoors)*   £62
30-minute lessons   £42

Please make all cheques payable to "Ian Poole" and hand them to him or post them to 114 Castle Lane, Olton, Solihull, B92 7AN.

We hope you enjoy your spring tennis!

The Junior Tennis Committee.
