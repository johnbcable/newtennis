REMINDER


Annual Junior Singles Tennis Tournament 2008


Two Age Groups


Under 12's (as at 1 January, 2008) - 1 pm, Sunday 22 June


Under 16's (as at 1 January, 2008) - 1 pm, Sunday 29 June


Entry fee £4 per person


Spectators are welcome to come and support the children - please come along and watch.

All junior members who are capable of full court rallying with full compression tennis balls, are invited to take part in the Annual Junior Tennis Tournament!.

This is a great opportunity to put into practice all the skills you have been developing.  So, come and play singles with your friends  at the Club!

Medals and trophies for runners up and winners.

Four categories

GIRLS and BOYS UNDER 12 (as at 1st January 2008) - 1 pm, Sunday 22nd June
GIRLS and BOYS UNDER 16 (as at 1st January 2008) - 1 pm, Sunday 29th June

This event is for MEMBERS ONLY.  If you aren't already a member, please contact Chester Barnes on 01675 443572 for details, go onto our website www.hamptontennis.org.uk, or pick up a membership application form from the tennis notice board in the club house.
