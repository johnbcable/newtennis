Three courts open

3 courts ready!

 We are delighted to announce that the three court block is ready for play, although not officially signed off.  As the club is not insured until the courts are signed off, YOU PLAY AT YOUR OWN RISK.  We will let you know shortly when all the courts are officially signed off for play.

 

Footwear

 It is VERY IMPORTANT that you wear tennis shoes or non marking trainers to play.  Definitely no running shoes, studs, blades, grooves or defined heels.  Incorrect footwear WILL significantly damage the courts. 

 

Floodlight tokens

Light tokens can be bought at the bar.  More information on this will be circulated soon.

 

Booking

The courts can be booked on the booking sheets pinned to the notice board.  Max 2 hours.
