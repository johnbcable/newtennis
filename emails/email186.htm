7 ladies who have entered the above competition still need partners. And we know you men are out there ...

The annual tournament organisers are ready to publish the draws for this year's annual torunament .... with the exception of the the above competiton. This year more ladies than men have entered this competition. This is great news in one way as more and more of our ladies are happy to take part in this competition, which usually turns out to be one of the most enjoyable of the tournament competitions both for participants and for supporters. See you on court then, boys!

The mixed doubles plate are not the uber-pairings of the main mixed doubles: this competition is for fun. All pairings are established so that ANY pair in this competition could realistically win - there are no outright favourites. If you enjoyed the Maxi-Tennis (or if you missed that and would like to take part in this more traditional format but fun competition) then please let Geoff (Redfern - 01675 442447) know if you wish to enter as soon as possible. Finals day this year is to be held on July 5th so please remember to put your name forward only if you will be around on that day .... and do it soon so the competition can commence!
