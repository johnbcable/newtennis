Your tournament entry choices

This year we are holding the Challenger and Junior Finals Day on on Saturday July 2nd to coincide with Village Festival weekend. The Open, Veterans, Mixed Plate and Mini Finals Day will be on Saturday September 17th.

The tournaments will take the same format as last year except that the Challenger and Junior tournaments will be run as a qualifying tournament for the Open tournaments. Challenger semi-finalists and junior finalists will qualify to go forward into the main tournament.

If you are in the top four mens and top two ladies Coventry league squads or you have played in the top two singles box leagues in 2015 you will be ineligible for the Challenger tournament and will therefore receive a bye into the main tournament draw.
