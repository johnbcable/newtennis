2009-10 Coaching Vision/Goal

Competition - Within the club we are looking to provide more in-house competition opportunities by creating friendly/fun matchplay and tournaments to build on the skills learnt during coaching sessions.  All competition results can be recorded and count toward your LTA British Tennis rating which you get by holding BTM (BRITISH TENNIS MEMBERSHIP).  If you are not signed up we strongly recommend you do as it is free of charge and has benefits such as accessing Wimbledon tickets. You can register (or check on your registration status) by going to our club web site.

When you have registered on British Tennis (or we have done that for you if you have given us permission), they will contact you directly with your account details and you can monitor your progress on their website.
