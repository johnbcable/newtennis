Summer 2010 Junior Coaching Programme

 

 

 

 

 

 

Monday 19 April to Saturday 24 July 2010

Excluding:

  • Play Tennis Day - Saturday 1 May

  • May Day - Monday 3 May

  • Half-term - 31 May to 5 June

The cold weather is finally receding and we hope all our existing juniors plus new starters and returners are looking forward to the new tennis season.

 

Please find attached, a schedule and a booking form for the Summer 2010 junior coaching programme. The costs and also discounts and surcharges are given below.

 

All juniors aged 7 or over on 1 April 2010 must be members of the tennis club. However, attendees who are new to the courses can play for half a term without being members whilst they decide whether tennis at Hampton is for them. For more information on joining the tennis club, please visit our web site

 

Juniors aged under 7 on 1 April 2010 have to be registered on the club database. There is no charge for this.

 

The tennis courses recommence on Monday 19 April and will run for 13 weeks. However, please note that Saturday courses will not run on Play Tennis Day (1 May 2010) and Monday courses will not run on Bank Holiday Monday (3 May) and therefore will be for only 12 weeks. The costs for the term are as follows:

  Tues, Wed & Thurs Sats & Mons
1-hour lessons £65 £60
45 minutes £52 £48
30 minutes £39 £36

In order to encourage prompt payment, which makes administration much easier, there are discounts as shown below for those who pay their termly fee by 10 April. Payment can be made during your lesson or by post to Ian's home address, as shown on the attached booking form.

 

Chasing payment is time consuming and tedious. There will be a surcharge for late payments not received by the 3rd lesson of term.

  Prompt Payment Discount Late Payment Surcharge
1-hour lessons £5 £5
45 minutes £4 £4
30 minutes £3 £3

 

Returners and new starters:

If you would like to see if there is a space for you on one of our courses, please contact Ian.

 

Non-Returners:
