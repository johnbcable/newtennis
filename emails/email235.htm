Funkydory are back!

funkdory

once again with special guest

Dr Ika

Saturday 29 November, 2008
at the Clubhouse, Shadowbrook Lane
8 'til late

Cool jazz/ blues/funk band funkydory return again to Hampton In Arden to kick start the run-in to Christmas. The brain-child of guitarist Jules Fothergill and his wife Lorna Reilly (lead vocals), the 4-piece band are completed with Hayden Doyle on drums and Ian Mauracio on bass. If you haven't got into this band yet, then you may be able to snap up a copy of their first album, "Still", at the gig.

Hailing from Georgia in the ex-USSR, Dr Ika became a favourite at record label Blues Matters before teaming up with our very own Peter Morris in a venture specialising in high quality amps and speaker cabinets. Sporting a vast range of guitar styles from delicate finger picking through to raucous, Jimi Hendrix covers, Dr Ika is a consumate guitarist who any budding player could learn a thing or two from!
