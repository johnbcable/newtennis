BBQ - Wimbledon Mens Singles Finals Day
Sunday 3 July

 
We warmly invite members, their friends and families to join us for an informal BBQ at the clubhouse this Sunday where you can watch the Wimbledon Finals with lots of other people who love tennis!

 

We will light the BBQ at 1 pm. Please bring your own BBQ food along to cook yourself. We have a cooker if its raining, so don't be put off by the weather!

 

Social tennis as well for anyone who is interested so don't forget your racquet
