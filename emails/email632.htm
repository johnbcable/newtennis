Tots Tennis Coaching Programme

A message from our new Tots tennis coach – Cassie Boneham.

Good afternoon,

I hope you have all had a great summer and your children are looking forward to playing tennis again in September.

I have made some slight changes to how Tots Tennis will run commencing Saturday 8th Septemberspan>. There will be 2 main Tots sessions as follows:

Tiny Aces Tots Tennis

Tiny Aces is aimed at children aged 2-3 years old learning fun based tennis activities through fundamental areas such as coordination, movement and ball skills- which are the essential foundations for tennis. The programme is designed to develop the children’s sense of hand-eye coordination, to hold and swing a racket and importantly to run around and have lots of fun!

Little Aces Tots Tennis

Little Aces is a great introduction to racket and ball sports. Focused at children aged 4-5 years old aiming to improve their ABC’s (Agility, Balance, Coordination and Speed), the programme is designed to get young children active and learning new skills in an exciting and social environment. Tennis is great fun to learn yet difficult to master, so the earlier you start your ‘Little Ace’ the better!

Session times and payment:

Saturdays

  • 9.45-10.15am Tiny Aces (2-3 year olds, £4 per child- pay as you play)
  • 10.30-11.00am Little Aces (3-5 year olds, £4 per child- pay as you play)

Please let me know which sessions you require as I am having a ratio of 2 coaches to 6 children, still aiming for the dynamic of 1 coach to 3 children during the sessions. I can be flexible with the timing of the sessions and even add more during the day to make sure they are suitable for you and your child.

If you would like to contact me please do not hesitate to do so. Also if you would like to give me some feedback that would be greatly appreciated. You can contact me by email on cassie@littleaces.co.uk or by phone on 07864363605.

Thank you for taking the time to read the new start of Little Aces and Tiny Aces Tots Tennis. I am excited to get started and looking forward to meeting yourselves and the children.
