Wimbledon Men’s Final BBQ and Children’s Tennisathon

1pm  Sunday 4 July 2004

There’s plenty going on in the sporting world at the moment – Wimbledon and Euro 2004.

We invite you to come and enjoy the atmosphere with friends at the Tennis Club and watch these exciting sporting events.

The action starts at 1.00 pm with a drink of punch and BBQ/salad, and strawberries and cream.  Tickets cost just £5 for adults and £2.50 for children.

 There will also be LIVE action to watch – the juniors will be completing a sponsored tennisathon from 1pm to 4pm.  This involves non-stop tennis for the whole three hours, starting with the tots and going right through the age groups to teenagers.  The children are trying to raise money for the tennis court refurbishment fund, so please come and cheer them on!

 Don’t forget your racket - social tennis for all from 4pm.
