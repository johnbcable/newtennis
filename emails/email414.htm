Wimbledon Ticket Draw

Friday 6th April, 2012

The draw for Wimbledon ticket pairs will be made on Friday 6th April at the Clubhouse at 7.30pm.

The list of members to be included in the draw at the moment are:

Chris Barnes, Maria Barnes, Alexander Barnes, Ray Barnett, Chris Durbin, Katerina Gaspar, Savio Gaspar, Cameron Grant, Carol Grant, Mike Jones, Simon Jevons, Sally Leach, Stewart Maudsley, Brett McSkimming, Wayman Morris, Jenny Roberts, Christopher Thomas, Paul Thomas

If you are not in the list of members above and you would like to enter the draw please reply to this email by Thursday April 5th. You must be a tennis member, at least 10 years old on June 25th and a BTM member. Draw winners will receive a pair of tickets and are free to choose any guest to take with them, although under 16's must be accompanied by an adult. Tickets cost from £64 and £220 a pair.

Please note that under LTA rules, tickets are not transferable so if you cannot go you should let us know quickly so that we can reallocate the tickets to a member on the reserve list. If you do not follow this rule you run the risk of jeopardising the club's future ticket allocation.

Thank you for your assistance.
