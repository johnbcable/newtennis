Summer 2010 Holiday Camps At Lady Katherine Leveson School, Temple Balsall, Knowle B93 0AN

 

 

 

Summer 2010 Holiday Camps
Monday 26th - Wednesday 28th July
Monday 2nd - Wednesday 4th August
Monday 9th - Wednesday 11th August.

 

 

 

 

These camps are for juniors aged 4 to 13. So come along, have fun, learn new skills and get some excercise!

Invasion games
Net games
Striking and fielding games
Racket games
Multi-skills
Dance
Gymnastics


 

The camps will run for 3 days on the first 3 weeks of the holidays and will begin at 9:30am and finish at 3:30pm, using Lady Katherine Leveson School playground, field and school hall for activities. There will be childcare from 8:30am, at an extra cost of £3, but this will depend upon numbers.

 

The cost of the camp will be £20 per child per day and children will be required to bring their own lunch. We will, however, be offering weekly rates and rates for siblings. If you book your child in for the full 3 days the cost will be £50. Discounts are available for family bookings, for example, if you book more than one child in the cost will be £18 per child per day.

 

This is an exciting and worthwhile opportunity, during which the children can develop and enhance new and existing skills, whilst having fun.
