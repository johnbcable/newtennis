Coventry League promotions

Congratulations to the Mens 2nd and 3rd teams and to the ladies 2nd team who have been promoted. No teams were relegated.

For the Mens 2nd team it was a fourth consecutive promotion (wow) this time as Champions of division 2. Next season they will play in division 1 in the same division as the first team.

For the Mens 3rd team it was an unbeaten season as Champions of division 5.

For the Ladies 2nd team it was a second consecutive promotion to take them into division 4.

Finally and for the second year running, commiserations to the Mens 1st team who last year were relegated (just) from the premier league on "goal" difference. This year they finished third on 45 points in division 1 which must be a record number of points for a team finishing third.
