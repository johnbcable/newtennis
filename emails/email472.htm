Wimbledon Tickets - 2013 Draw Results

The results of the draw conducted at the Sports Club on Friday 12th April are shown below. If your name is on the list, you have paid your 2013/14 subscription, are at least 10 years old on June 25th and a BTM member then the process for obtaining your tickets is as follows:

  • Saturday April 20th. - You must by this date have sent or dropped off a cheque payable to "Hampton-in-Arden Sports Club" to Wayman Morris at Shadowbrook, Shadowbrook Lane, Hampton-in-Arden, Solihull B92 0DQ. Wayman lives on Shadowbrook Lane directly opposite the back of the Sports Club with a steep in and out drive. If you have not paid for your tickets by this date we will assume that you do not wish to take up your ticket allocation;

  • Once we have received the tickets from the LTA we will inform you by email of the arrangements for collection from Wayman;

  • Tickets that have not been taken up will be offered by email to members on the reserve list.

Guests: - The named person in the draw may nominate a guest of their own choosing to who does not have to be a member of the tennis club, provided that if the named person is under 16 he or she must be accompanied by an adult guest. There is no need to let the club know the name of the guest.

Non transferability: - Under LTA rules the tickets are NOT transferable. This means that if the named member cannot go, the ticket allocation must be returned to the club to be offered to a member drawn on the reserve list. Please note that if you do not follow this rule you run the risk of jeopardising the club's future ticket allocation.

If you do not wish to take up your allocation, please let Wayman know as soon as possible to help us to find homes for all the tickets which are allocated to the club. Wayman can be contacted on 01675 442071 or at waymanmorris@mail.com.

Thank you for your assistance
