Hampton-in-Arden Tennis Club

REMINDER - Finals Day

Finals Day is Saturday 4 July, 2009

Play is scheduled to start at 12pm.

Everyone is invited to attend our Club's main competitive event of the tennis season - Club Finals Day. Please come and watch some great tennis and support the players. Seating is limited so we would advise you to bring portable seats if you have them.

Trophies will be presented at around 6pm dependent upon completion of the matches.

A detailed order of play will be put on the Tennis Notice Board on Friday evening.

If you are involved in a final, a member of the tournament committee will phone you on Friday evening to advise you of the approximate timing of your match(es).

Pimms and other refreshments will be on offer during the afternoon. Volunteers to bring and serve afternoon tea, cakes, strawberries & cream, etc would be greatly appreciated. If you are able to help, please reply to this email.

There will also be a BBQ later in the day.

 

Finally, "Soul Matters", a very talented group of young musicians from the area, return to our club and will be performing from 7.30pm. Tickets are available from the bar priced at £5.

Looking forward to seeing you for an action-packed day!

Don't forget: the league tennis season is in full flight. All our home matches (Ladies and Mens) are listed in the Event Diary. So, check it out, come along to the clubhouse and support our teams on our new courts while enjoying refreshments from the bar.

Keep your eyes on your email's and the Tennis Club web Event Diary for more events (when we list them!).
