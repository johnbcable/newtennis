4 Days To Go!

Davis Cup Generation Tournament

Saturday 11th June, 2016
From 10.30am to 2.30pm

LATEST NEWS: Star from Aegon tennis tournament to join the celebrations in the afternoon - don't miss her visit! ALSO: school children signing-up for Davis Cup tournament.

Come along dressed as your favourite tennis lookalike (through the ages !), for what promises to be a fun–filled event starting with a great warm up from 10:30am courtesy of our coaching staff (including ball machines, serve speed to timers, inflatables etc.). Prizes will be awarded for the best costumes!

Fifty places in total available. Please email Ian Poole on ianpoole23@yahoo.co.uk or text 07968 451956 to secure your place. £5 entry per person. Full ball kids, teenagers and all adults welcome.

Prizes for best Davis Cup lookalike will be awarded starting at 2.30pm. Fancy dress is optional but this is your chance to buy a limted-edition 50th anniversary T–shirt and wear it on the day.

BBQ – Courtesy of Andy Frith from 12 noon till 3pm - enjoy his culinary delights whilst watching or playing in the Davis Cup generation tennis tournament.

Thanks for your support and see you on court!
