Club Clothing - Orders Now Being Taken

 

 

 

 

We are now taking orders for new high-quality sports clothing. The garments are available for both adults and children and will be on display at the club as follows:

 

10.15 - 12.30 and 1.45 - 2.30,   Saturday 1 May (Club Open Day)

3.45 - 5.00  Mondays, Tuesdays and Wednesdays until 12 May

                      (but excluding Bank Holiday Monday)

7.00 - 8.00 pm   Fridays (prior to 12 May)

 

 

 

Check out the full catalogue at  http://www.hamptontennis.org.uk/clubsportswear.asp

 

An order form is attached giving details of styles, prices and sizes available. You can print this to use as your order form or you can pick up a form during the viewing times listed above. Please note that we cannot accept returns, so please ensure what you are ordering is the correct size. There will be samples of various sizes available during the viewings.

 

Payment should be made by cheque payable to "Hampton In Arden Sports Club".

 

Orders must be received by Saturday 15 May. We will then place a bulk order to the supplier. It will not be possible to make further orders after this date.

 

We do hope you will like our new club clothing.
