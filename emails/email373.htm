Summer 2011 Junior Coaching Programme

Fees Reminder

Monday 25 April to Saturday 23 July 2011

(excluding the day of the Royal Wedding, 29 April & half term week, beginning 30 May)

Thanks to those of you who have paid this term's coaching fees.

However, there are still some outstanding fees and if this applies to you we would be grateful if these could be paid as soon as possible as the new term started in April and the fees are now overdue.

Please find attached, a schedule and a booking form for the Summer junior coaching programme. The costs are given below:

1-hour lessons £66.00
1-hour lessons (Fridays)* £61.00
45-minute lessons £52.00
45-minute lessons (Fridays)* £48.00
30-minute lessons £40.00

Please hand your payment to Ian or post to 114 Castle Lane, Olton, Solihull, B92 7AN.
Please make all cheques payable to

Matchpoint Sportz Ltd

Sportz is spelt with a "z".

Payment can also be made direct to bank. Please contact Ian for further details.

If you are not planning to return, please can you let Ian know by the end of term in person or via mobile: 07968 451956. It is really important for Ian to know how many children will be on the programme as he needs to maintain suitable coach to player ratios.

All juniors aged 7 or over on 1 April 2011 must be members of the tennis club. However, attendees who are new to the courses can play for half a term without being members whilst they decide whether tennis at Hampton is for them. For more information on joining the tennis club, please click here.

Juniors aged under 7 on 1 April 2011 have to be on the club database. There is no charge for this.
