REMINDER Spring Tennis and Multi-Sport Camps

We are running our popular tennis and multi sport camps again this spring half-term holiday.

Numbers on the camps are limited.
Please book by Wednesday 10th February by email to secure your place.

Tennis Camps
Monday 15th February,
Tuesday 16th February,
Wednesday 18th February.

The above camps are aimed at children aged 6 and over. Camps all run from 9.30am to 3.30pm and cost £20.00 (reduced to £18.00 if paid by Monday 8th February). Early drop offs from 8.30 am, £3.50 extra per day. Remember to bring a drink and a packed lunch.

Special offer – £50 for all three days if paid by 8th February.

Tots Camps
Monday 15th February,
Tuesday 16th February,
Wednesday 18th February.

These camps are aimed at children aged under 6. Each camp will run from 9.30am to 12.30 and cost £11 per child.

Special offer – £27 for all three days if paid by 8th February.

Please note that booking AND pre-payment are ESSENTIAL for all camps so that we can ensure we have adequate staff available. Bookings will only be accepted with an online transfer or cheque/cash payment to Ian.

If paying on the day, the tennis camps will cost £22 and the Tots camps will cost £13 and we CANNOT guarantee that we will be able to accommodate your child.

Interested? Please email ianpoole23@yahoo.co.uk (with brief details of who will attend and when) to secure your place as early as possible. Please also remember to make sure you bring completed booking forms along at the first attendance. For information about bookings or any other information please contact Ian Poole on 07968 451956 or via email to ianpoole23@yahoo.co.uk.

Please Remember: bookings are ONLY secured with payment.

A booking form is attached for you to print and complete.
