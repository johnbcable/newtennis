New for 2004: Mini-Tennis Awards Scheme 
Friday March 5th from 6:45pm 
Junior Parents Social Evening 

Following on from the club's successful entry into Mini-Tennis activities, we are pleased to announce that we shall be giving our youngsters the chance to take part in the Lawn tennis Association Mini Tennis Awards scheme.

In common with swimming awards, these are not primarily a competitive device but rather a way of recognising and further developing each child's skills. Incorporated into existing junior coaching sessions, children will find them easy to understand and hopefully exciting and rewarding to undertake.

For further information, come along to the above meeting and speak with members of the Junior Committee on the night. Alternatively, if you cannot make the meeting, contact Ro Eccleston (01675) 442913, Jayne Barnes (01675) 443572 or Jane Hodge (01675 443197) (mobile: 07900 025718)
