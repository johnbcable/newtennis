Tennis Events and Dates for the Diary

Welcome to the 2015 tennis season (4 of 4)

Dear members and prospective members,

A brief summary of tennis and social events for you this season is set out below. More details will follow by email during the course of the season.

External Competitive Tennis

  • – AEGON team tennis: One mens team, one mens veterans team (35+) and a number of junior teams playing at weekends from April to June.
  • – Coventry League: Five mens teams and three ladies teams playing on weekday evenings from the last week of April to the first week in August.
  • – Warwickshire Winter veterans (45+) leagues: One mens team (new this year) and one ladies team playing at weekends from September to February.

Internal Competitive Tennis

  • – Box leagues: Adult and Junior singles and adult doubles box leagues are run throughout the year with 6 or 7 players (or teams) in each box and promotion and relegation at the end of each 10 week playing period. Each match lasts for about one hour.
  • – Open Tournaments: the competition runs from May to September with two finals days - Saturday July 4 for the Challenger Tournament and Sunday September 27 for the Annual Tournament. Note that the Challenger semi-finalists and junior finalists qualify to go forward into the Annual Tournament draw.
  • – Veterans Tournaments: this is run for mens and ladies doubles for 40+ and 55+ age groups with the finals played on the September 27 finals day.
  • – Junior and Mini Tournaments: qualifying events to determine the finalists are run by the coaching team over a weekend to be publicised. Some of the finals will be played on the July 4 finals day and the remaining finals will be played on the September 27 finals day.

Social Tennis

The programme is set up to ensure that social tennis is available to all playing levels:

  • – Monday 7.30 to 9pm: Team players (men)*
  • – Tuesday 8 to 10pm: Intermediates (mixed)
  • – Thursday 7 to 8pm: Team players (ladies)*
  • – Friday 8 to 10pm: Intermediates (mixed)
  • – Saturdays 3 to 5pm: Families (mixed)
  • – Sunday 10.30 to 12.30am: Intermediates (mixed)

N.B. asterisked items (*) above only run outside the Coventry League tennis season which runs from late April to early August each year.

If you would like help to decide which session to attend please call Ian Poole on 07968 451956

Coaching

Ian Poole and his team of Sam Peace, Bel Chefri, Sam Hiskett and Zach Jones run a full programme of adult and junior individual and group lessons throughout the year in school term times. There are summer camps and other coaching opportunities outside normal term time. Please contact Ian Poole on 07968 451956 or have a look at the web site for more information.

Dates for your Diary

  • – Apr 10 Friday – Wimbledon ballot draw
  • – Apr 25 Saturday – Quiz
  • – Jun 21 Sunday – Arden 9 Road Race
  • – Jul 4 Saturday – Challenger and Junior Finals Day (also Village Festival)
  • – Jul 10, 11 Friday/Saturday – Family trip to Wimbledon
  • – Sep 27 Sunday – Open, Veterans and Mini Finals Day
  • – Nov 23 Monday – Tennis AGM
  • – Jan 15 (2016) Friday – Annual Presentation Dinner and Dance at Hampton Manor


We hope you have an enjoyable and successful season.
