REMINDER: Coaching Fees

Many thanks to all of you who have paid your autumn term coaching fees.

However, there are still some outstanding fees and we would be grateful if these could be paid as soon as possible as the new term started at the beginning of September and the fees are now overdue.

Please find attached, a schedule and a booking form for the Autumn junior coaching programme. The costs are given below:

1-hour lessons £76
45-minute lessons £61
30-minute lessons £45

All juniors aged 7 or over on 1 April 2010 must be members of the tennis club. However, attendees who are new to the courses can play for half a term without being members whilst they decide whether tennis at Hampton is for them. For more information on joining the tennis club, please visit our web site.

Juniors aged under 7 on 1 April 2010 have to be registered on the club database. There is no charge for this.

Payment can be made during your lesson or by post to Ian's home address, as shown on the attached booking form.

Thank you for your assistance,
