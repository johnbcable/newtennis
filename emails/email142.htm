Adult Tennis Coaching Returns

Do you have some spare time during the day or can you make time on some Thursday evenings from early June? Why not kick-start an improvement in your tennis skills? Come along to one of these sessions and develop your technique during the 2007 season.

All of the following sessions are run by our LTA licensed tennis professional, Alan Cutts.

Thursday Mornings

All adults - all standards 9.30 - 10.30 am
Continue to develop your game alongside your friends, play matches with them and have our coach give you advice to improve your game. Includes coffee. Cost per person is £5 per session

Thursday Evenings: 8 - 9.15 pm

The "Big Hit" returns
A more advanced programme to help you fine tune your game. Sessions run on 7th, 21st, 28th (June) and 12th and 19th (July). Venue:  at Eversfield School, Solihull

Cost for the 5 weeks is £27 (pounds)  £35 (pounds non - members). There are 8 places available

Please email Alan before Wed the 6th June if you wish to attend (alan@coaching.ac)

Friday (early afternoons)

All standards adult mix-in sessions 2.00 - 3.00 pm
All standards of play are welcome to come along and have our coach advise you on your game. Cost is £5 per session

Special offers on group bookings for Thursday morning and Friday (early) afternoon sessions. Contact Alan for details. To book your session(s), or for more information, help and advice, contact Alan on 07748 656002 or via alan@coaching.ac
