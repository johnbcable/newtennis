Easter 2011 Tennis Camps at Hampton

9.30am - 12.30pm Monday 11th & 18th April - Mini Tennis Camps

9.30am - 3.00pm Wednesday 13th - Full Court Tennis/Competition Day

We are offering these activities to both members and non-members.

Mini Tennis Camps

These camps are for red, orange and green mini tennis players aged 3 to 10. Activities will include drills, skills, matchplay/competition.

Cost £10 members, £13.50 non-members

Please bring a drink, and also a snack for break time.

Full Court Tennis/Competition Day

This is for any junior playing green tennis and above.

Programme:

  • 9.30 - 11.00Warm up and drills
  • 11.00 - 11.20-- Break --
  • 11.20 - 12.20Video analysis and technical work
  • 12.20 - 13.00-- Lunch --
  • 13.00 - 15.00Competition / Match Play

Cost £13.50 members, £16.00 non-members

Please bring a drink and a packed lunch.

For bookings or any other information please contact Ian Poole:

0796 8451956 or ianpoole23@yahoo.co.uk.

Booking is essential to ensure the correct coach to student ratio can be planned. We appreciate your assistance with this.
