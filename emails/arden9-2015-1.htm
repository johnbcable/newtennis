K & D Arden 9-Mile Road Race
(supported by Birmingham Runner)

Sunday 21 June 2015 - 10.30am
Hampton-in-Arden Sports Club B92 0DQ

Thank you very much for taking part in previous Arden 9 mile road races. The 2015 Arden 9 mile road race will once again be hosted by Knowle & Dorridge Running Club and has also been accepted as part of the 2015 Warwickshire Road Race League.

Online entries only please - through MyRaceApp or Runners World (maximum of 400 entrants in total):

Via MyRaceApp


Register »

Via Runners World


Register »

Advance entry fee £11 (for UK Athletics affiliated club members) or £13 otherwise.

On the day entry will be available if the race is not full by then.

No credit card fees.


A technical t-shirt will be awarded to all finishers and trophies or presentation medals to category winners.

2015 race category prizes will be awarded in line with WRRL competition categories, namely:- Overall (first 3), 35-44, 45-54 and 55+. (Each individual athlete will only be eligible for one individual prize).

Teams comprise 4 runners for Men and 3 runners for Ladies.

Please note: the use of MP3 players (or equivalent) during the race is strictly prohibited for your own safety as the roads on the route are not all closed and it is crucial that you are able to hear all safety briefings and marshall's instructions.

Please also note that there is a strict 2 hour time limit.

You can find out more information on:

  • Knowle and Dorridge Running Club at www.knowleanddorridgerunningclub.org.uk »
  • the Warwickshire Road Race League at www.warwickshirecountyaa.co.uk »

As in previous years the race is intended for both regular runners and novices and will be approved by the Midland Counties Athletic Association as a race to be held under the current laws of competition of the Amateur Athletic Association of England and in accordance with the bye-laws of the Midland Counties Athletic Association. The course has been measured as nine miles by the UK Athletics Course Measurement Scheme.
