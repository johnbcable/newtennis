Coventry League promotion

Congratulations to the Mens 1st team for winning promotion back into the Coventry league premier division. We are looking forward to watching more top class tennis on Monday evenings next summer.

Well done also to the Mens 2nd team who had a great season finishing third in division 1 after four consecutive promotions.

Not such good news elsewhere unfortunately. The Mens 3rd team slipped back into division 5 after one season in division 4 while the Ladies 2nd team also slipped back into division 5 after one season in division 4.
