Hampton-In-Arden Tennis Club

STOP PRESS!

Cancellation of Court Usage

 and Tennis Coaching
 

14 to 16 May 2004

 

This note confirms the work scheduled by the Parish Council on the sports ground car park WILL GO AHEAD.  Work will commence after 1.00 pm on Friday 14 May and continue until the evening of Sunday 16 May.  Although the Club House will be open as usual, the tennis courts will not be available during this period. 

ALL tennis coaching sessions for Friday, Saturday and Sunday have been cancelled as a consequence.  These sessions will resume next weekend, from Friday 21 May.  Deductions will be made from the next session’s course fees in respect of the cancelled lessons. 

We apologise for the inconvenience, but look forward to having a better car park!
