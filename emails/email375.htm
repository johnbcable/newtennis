Annual Junior Tournaments 2011

Finals to be played on Sat 2 July 2011

Entry fee: £4

Juniors are invited to enter our annual club tournaments. This is a brilliant way to use your tennis skills therefore we do hope you will enter! Supporters are welcome, so please come and encourage the children. There will be medals for winners and runners-up. The finals will be played on the Senior and Junior Club Finals Day on Saturday 2 July, 2011. Finalists must be available for this date, please.

Tournament Age Time and Date
Mini-Red Boys and Girls Born 2003 or later 1.00pm to 3.30pm, Sat 11 June
Mini-Orange Boys and Girls Born 2002, 2003 or 2004 1.00pm to 3.30pm, Sat 11 June
Mini-Green Boys and Girls
+ Advanced Mini Orange 		Born 2001 or 2002 1.00pm to 3.00pm, Sun 12 June
     
U12 Boys Under 12 on 1 Jan 2011 4.00pm to 6.00pm, Sun 12 June
U12 Girls Under 12 on 1 Jan 2011 4.00pm to 6.00pm, Sun 12 June
U16 Boys Under 16 on 1 Jan 2011 3.30pm to 5.30pm, Sat 11 June
U16 Girls Under 16 on 1 Jan 2011 3.30pm to 5.30pm, Sat 11 June

Please note: The above competitions are open to all junior members except those with a LTA rating of 9.2 or lower. However, the U16 Boys and U16 Girls competitions are open to any members of that age group. If you are not sure which mini-tennis colour band you belong to, please speak to Ian for guidance or go to our web site and fill in your date of birth for a quick check.

Please don't forget participants who are aged 7 or older as at 1 April 2011 must be MEMBERS of the club. Younger participants must be registered on the club data base. If you wish to know more, contact Chester Barnes on , go onto our website www.hammptontennis.org.uk, or pick up a membership application form from the tennis notice board in the club house.

Bad weather on tournament day?
If the weather is uncertain, please phone Ian (07968 451956) to check if it is still going to take place.
