Adult Tennis Sessions

Need inspiration to get back on court? You may be interested in some of our new adult tennis sessions:

Assess your needs
On Thursday 17 and 24 January, 7.30-9.00pm, there will be two pay as you play tennis coaching sessions to assess your goals and aims for either group or individual lessons. Pay £6 for an hour and get half an hour free. Regular lessons then to commence from the following week, starting Thursday 31 January.


Cardio Tennis
This is a fun way to get into shape and burn off some Christmas pudding! Cardio tennis is a group activity involving a warm-up, cardio workout and a cool down. It is suitable for both experienced players and beginners. So get on court and give this cardio work out a go.

Weekly sessions are available starting next Wednesday, 16 January 9.30 - 10.30 am. The cost is £5 per session.
