Big Racket/Geoff Redfern disco

Thank you to all who supported The Big Racket and the Geoff Redfern disco fund raiser on March 31st. 

You will pleased to know that apart from giving everyone a great time, the event raised nearly £736, all of which will be put into the tennis court refurbishment fund.

Thanks to everyone who made the evening possible.  Some names just have to be mentioned - starting with the band who have been practising each week for the past few months - so thanks to Alan Harding, Glyn Ryland, Guy Hemington, Joe Killeen and John Cable - you were brilliant!

Also thank you to Geoff Redfern for once again providing sound backup for the band and for a great disco to warm us up and to follow The Big Racket 

Finally thank you to all of you who helped to sell tickets, set the room up and tidy it afterwards and of course to man the bar in the evening

Don't forget: Keep your eyes on your email and the Tennis Club web Event Diary for more events during 2007
