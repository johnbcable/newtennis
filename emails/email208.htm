Reminder


Finals Day is Saturday 5 July, 2008


Play is scheduled to start at 12pm with the Junior U16 finals.

Trophies will be presented at around 6pm dependent upon completion of the matches.

A detailed order of play will be put on the Tennis Notice Board (and web site if provided to the web administrator!) on Friday evening.

If you are involved in a final, a member of the tournament committee will phone you on Friday evening to advise you of the approximate timing of your match(es).

There will not be a BBQ this year but any volunteers to bring and serve afternoon tea, cakes, strawberries & cream, etc greatly appreciated.

Don't forget: Keep your eyes on your email's and the Tennis Club web Event Diary for more events (when we list them!). Also, the league tennis season has started. Check out our Event Diary to see when our teams are playing at home. Come along to the clubhouse and support our teams while enjoying a drink from the bar.
