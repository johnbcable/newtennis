FINAL REMINDER - JUNIOR SINGLES COMPETITION!

Annual Junior Tennis Tournament

Under 13 (as at 1 January 2008)

Sunday 17 February 2008,

1 pm onwards

Entry Fee £4

Prizes for winners and runners up, sponsored by Widney Sports

All junior members who are aged 13 or under (as at 1 January 2008) and who are capable of full court rallying with full compression tennis balls, are invited to take part in the Annual Junior Tennis Tournament.

This is a great opportunity to put into practice all the skills you have been developing.  So come and play singles with your friends at the Club!

Prizes, medals and trophies for runners up and winners. 

Please let Ian know as soon as possible if you are entereing.

Two categories:

BOYS UNDER 13 (as at 1st January 2008)

GIRLS UNDER 13 (as at 1st January 2008)

This event is for MEMBERS ONLY.  If you aren't already a member, please contact Chester Barnes on for details, go onto our website www.hamptontennis.org.uk, or pick up a membership application form from the tennis notice board in the club house.
