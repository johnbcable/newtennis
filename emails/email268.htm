Wimbledon Ticket Draw 

 

The draw for Wimbledon ticket pairs will be made on Friday 22nd May at the Clubhouse at 7.30pm. 

Are you:

1) 8 years old or over on June 20th 2009?

2) prepared to pay between £60 and £150 for a pair of tickets?

3) a member of British Tennis?

 and 4) a paid up member Hampton-in-Arden Tennis Club for 2009/10?

 

If you have answered yes to all four questions then you are eligable to be included in the draw. 

 

Members who ticked the box on the application form to say 'yes' are shown below.  If you are not on the list and you would like to be, simply reply to this email by Thursday 21st May.  Don't worry if you are not sure if you a member of British Tennis - we can ensure that you are enrolled.  However you must have paid your tennis subscription before the draw takes place.

 

Junior members who are at least 8 years old are allowed to enter the draw this year for the first time.  Juniors under 16 must be accompanied to Wimbledon by an adult.
