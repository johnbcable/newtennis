Tournament Draw 2011

now incl. Mixed Doubles Plate

The full 2011 tournament draw - now including the mixed doubles plate is attached. It is also on display on the web site and the tennis club notice board.

Deadlines - The deadlines for playing matches are as follows:

  • Last 16 games - Sunday May 22nd
  • Quarter Finals - Sunday June 5th
  • Semi Finals - Sunday June 19th
  • Finals day is Saturday July 2nd

Please start playing your matches as soon as possible, especially if it is a last 16 match.

Booking a court - Courts can be booked on the new on line booking system. You can check availability and book courts from your home pc or from the terminal in the clubhouse situated in the corridor by the squash court door. You can find the site by going to the tennis club home page www.hamptontennis.org.uk, clicking on the "Book a court" link and then logon with your User Id and PIN. If you need help or you have forgotten your User Id or PIN please either refer to the email sent to you on March 17th 2011 or email support@hamptontennis.org.uk.
