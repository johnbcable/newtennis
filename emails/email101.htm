Hampton-In-Arden Tennis Club

Completion of Tournament Matches

Due to the number of tournament games that need to be played in a relatively short space of time, it has been agreed that (for the next three weeks only) Court 1 can be booked for tournament matches on Sunday mornings on the basis that the “Club” session rarely occupies more than two courts. 

In the event that more than eight people turn up to the Club Session and a tournament match has been booked, the tournament match will take priority and the Club Session will adopt the normal mix-in approach. Should this occurs, please play shortened sets so that players are not waiting too long.  

In addition, the Court Booking sheets have been reviewed making more courts available where coaching is not using all three courts. 

The tournament is progressing but there are still a lot of matches to be played in the next 3 weeks! 

Please take the initiative, arrange your matches and get them played out of courtesy to the rest of the members!

Stephen Ray
Chairman
