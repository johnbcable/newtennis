2009 Annual Junior Club Tournaments

Finals to be played on Sat 4 July 2009

Entry Fee £4

Juniors are invited to enter our annual club tournaments.  This is a brilliant way to use your tennis skills so please enter!   Supporters are welcome, to encourage the children. 

There will be medals for winners and runners-up.  The finals will be played on the Senior and Junior Club Finals Day on Saturday 4 July.  Finalists must be available for this date, please.
