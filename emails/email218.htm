Knowle Fun Run - Update on Fund Raising


Many thanks to everyone who took part earlier this year in the Knowle Fun Run after registering with either myself or Sue Kiely. As you may remember, this year the Lillie Johnson Charitable Trust kindly pledged £50 per runner to go towards our nominated charities (which this year were Hampton-In-Arden Recreational Trust and St Mary's Hospice, Selly Oak).

76 Runners - £4271.00


Well, this year you outdid yourselves! Not only did we send 76 Hampton runners into the race (a huge increase over last year's 44) but these runners helped us raise a massive £4271.00 - which has been split between our two charities. Well done everyone - hope we can see you again next year!

Karen Finch
