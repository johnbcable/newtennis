Social Tennis 2012 Season

We are delighted to announce a new social tennis evening on Thursdays from 8pm to 10pm specifically aimed at Improvers/Rusty Rackets, starting on March 8th.   For the first few weeks this will be attended by at least one member of the committee to make sure that the session runs smoothly. The full 2012 social tennis programme is shown below and is designed to make sure that there is a social tennis evening session available for all playing levels.

  • Mondays 8pm: Team players (men)
  • Tuesdays 8pm: Intermediates (mixed)
  • Thursdays 6.30pm: Team players (ladies)
  • Thursdays 8pm: Improvers/rusty rackets (mixed)- starting March 8th
  • Fridays 8pm: intermediates (mixed)
  • Sundays from 10am: intermediates (mixed)

Please can you make sure that you play to the standard intended for the evening? If you would like help to decide which session to attend please call Ian Poole on 07968 451956 for advice.
