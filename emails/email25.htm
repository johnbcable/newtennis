Changes - coaches

 


As you may be aware, unfortunately Jane will not be taking coaching sessions on Saturday mornings after half term.  Jane wishes to have more free time at the weekend and we hope she enjoys her Saturdays off. Jane has done a brilliant job over the past two years and we are all very grateful for her dedication and effort.  The good news is Jane will still be coaching the tots on Tuesdays and continuing to help out in other ways, for example, running holiday fun sessions.

 

Richard has also been here for two years and has decided to leave for pastures new.  The children will be very sad to loose their fun loving coach who has kept them keen to play even during the darkest, coldest winter evenings!  Anyone who would like to contribute to a collection for Richard should give their donations to Sarah White or to her nanny Nicky Mattey at the school gate.  Alternatively you can give them to Jayne Barnes, 68 Meriden Road, Hampton-in-Arden. 

 

Taking over both Friday and Saturday coaching sessions will be Caroline Clutterbuck.  Caroline lives in Knowle and has played tennis with passion since she was a small child.  Caroline is extremely enthusiastic and can’t wait to pass on her skills and knowledge to our juniors.   Caroline will start coaching the Saturday sessions next Saturday and after half term on Fridays.  We welcome Caroline to our Club and wish her success in her coaching career with us! 

Thank you.
The Junior Committee
