Key fobs and access to the club house

If you are a member of the Sports Club and wish to purchase a key fob to allow you access to the club house here is the process for you to follow:

"All members may purchase a key fob to allow access to the clubhouse through the outer front door at any time of the day. After 9:30pm the inner door will be locked and you will need to ring the bell to gain entry. The bar staff will release the lock after checking on the video monitor behind the bar that it is safe to do so. If you find yourself leaving at a time when there is no one else in the clubhouse please remember to lock all doors and windows before you leave so that security is not compromised. If you are 14 or over you may purchase a key fob from the bar on Tuesday evenings between 6pm and 9pm at a cost of £5 or alternatively have the key fob replaced free of charge in exchange for returning a faulty key fob. If you lose your key fob please would your report its loss to the bar on a Tuesday evening so that access to the clubhouse on that fob can be disabled to avoid compromising security."

Don't forget: Keep your eyes on your email's and the Tennis Club web Event Diary for more events (when we list them!)
