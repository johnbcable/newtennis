Ø       Your training is not going well?
Ø       Maybe you just can’t face the 9 mile run?

& let me tell you that beer is involved!

 Why not try the Arden Stagger instead
…….surely you have no excuse for missing this one?

 Here are the details:

Ø       Start from the Sports Club any time after 1pm after first registering with the “Race Director”.  You will be given a race number just like the Arden 9 runners and a list of questions to keep you alert around the course.

Ø       Why not enter as a team?   Bring the WHOLE FAMILY.

Ø       Walk or cycle the 6 mile course via the Malt Shovel and the Bull (both at Barston), the West Midlands Golf Club, the White Lion, the Engine and finally back to the Sports Club bar if you can make it.  The pubs are open all day from 12 noon.

Ø       No entry fee – we just ask you to make a donation to the Court Refurbishment Fund or maybe use your initiative to make as much money as you can.

Ø       If you want to buy a race medal you will need to show that you have been to all six watering holes by completing the quiz.

Ø       There will be a barbecue running from 11.30am to 1.30pm.

Ø       Team prizes are available for winners in the following categories:

o        Highest marks for the course quiz

o        Best outfit

o        Most money raised

 We take no responsibility for anyone found drunk in charge of a bicycle (or anything else for that matter!)

Thank you.
Chester Barnes (Secretary)
