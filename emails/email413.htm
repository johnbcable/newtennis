Coventry League Tennis

The league season starts in the week beginning Monday April 30th. Mens team practice is held on Mondays from 8pm to 10pm. Ladies team practice is held on Thursdays from 6.30pm to 8pm. If you want to be considered for selection for any of the league teams please come to practice nights over the next 5 weeks.

The list of fixtures for all our league teams can be found at http://hamptontennis.org.uk/league.asp.

For the weeks beginning Monday March 26th and Monday April 2nd the mens and ladies team team practices will be coached sessions. The coaching will be paid for by the club and is therefore free of charge to members. Please take advantage of this great opportunity to receive free coaching and to make sure that our teams are well prepared for the coming season.

Subscriptions due - April 1st

Please could everyone try their best to return the subscription cheques and membership forms by next weekend April 1st? Thank you.
