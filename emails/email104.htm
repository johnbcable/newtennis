JUNIOR SUMMER TENNIS

Junior Social Tennis

All junior members who are capable of full court rallying are eligible to attend the Junior Social Tennis session, held every Sunday 5 - 7 pm.  However, during the summer, this session will not be supervised, therefore younger members should be accompanied by parents/older members. 

Tennis Competitions

Over the summer, there are various tennis competitions.  Why not give it a go?  Invite some friends to go too.  The Beechwood competitions are particularly friendly.  (Mums and dads get cups of tea!)
