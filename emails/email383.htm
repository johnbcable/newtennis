Autumn 2011 Junior Coaching Programme

Monday 5 September to Saturday 17 December 2010

(excluding half term, week beginning 24 October)

Thank you to everyone who has supported the junior programme so far during 2011, including the children, parents/carers and volunteers. Also a big thank you to the coaches and assistants.

In preparation for next term, please find attached, a schedule and a booking form for the autumn 2011 junior coaching programme. The normal fees and also discounted and surcharged rates are given below.

All juniors aged 7 or over on 1 April 2011 must be members of the tennis club. However, attendees who are new to the courses can play for half a term without being members whilst they decide whether tennis at Hampton is for them. For more information on joining the tennis club, please visit our web site

Juniors aged under 7 on 1 April 2011 have to be registered on the club database. There is no charge for this.

The tennis courses recommence on Monday 5 September, in line with the start of the new school year. There are 14 weeks in the course and the coaching fees are as follows:

1-hour lessons £73.50
45-minute lessons £58.80
30-minute lessons £44.10

In order to encourage prompt payment, which makes administration much easier, there are discounts as shown below for those who pay their termly fee on or before the first lesson. Conversely, there will be a surcharge for late payments not received by the 3rd lesson of term. The amounts for prompt payment and also the amounts for late payments are as follows:..

  Prompt Payment
(If you pay on or before the first lesson) 		Late Payment
(If you pay after the third Autumn 2011 lesson)
1-hour lessons £70 £77
45-minute lessons £56 £61
30-minute lessons £42 £47

Please hand your payment to Ian or post to Flat 1, Bromford Mere, Warwick Road, Solihull, B92 7AN.
Please make all cheques payable to

Matchpoint Sportz Ltd

Sportz is spelt with a "z".

Payment can also be made direct to bank. Please contact Ian for further details.

To assist our planning, if you will not be returning to the courses in September, we would be grateful if you could let us know by replying to this email or by letting Ian know on: 07968 451956.

We wish you an enjoyable summer and look forward to seeing you back at lessons in September!

The Junior Tennis Committee.
