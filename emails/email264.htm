Hampton-In-Arden Tennis Club

Sports Club Open Day 16 May 2009

From 1.00 pm
Saturday 16 May 2009
at the club

We welcome you all to come along to our open day.

Hampton-in-Arden Tennis Club has hosted annual Play Tennis days for some years now and these have proved to be popular and well attended. This year in particular we have lots to celebrate as our new courts will soon be complete and ready for use. Bring family and friends; come and play some tennis, mini-squash and cricket and enjoy the afternoon. There will be members of the club and its committees around during the afternoon to help with any questions you may have. The basic layout of the afternoon is shown on the attached flyer.

Fancy helping out?
We will be serving tea and cakes during the afternoon. If there are any budding cake bakers out there who would be kind enough to make something tasty for the afternoon, we would be very grateful to hear from you. Also, if you could spare some time to help serve teas and coffees, we would be very pleased to hear from you too. If you are able to help please reply to this email or ring Jayne Barnes on .

Look forward to seeing you!

Don't forget: Keep your eyes on your email's and the Tennis Club web Event Diary for more events (when we list them!). Also, the league tennis season has started. Check out our Event Diary to see when our teams will be playing at home on our new courts. Come along to the clubhouse and support our teams while enjoying a drink from the bar.
