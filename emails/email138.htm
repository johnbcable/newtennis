URGENT: Finals Day _ Change of Date

Change of Finals Day of Club Tournaments

 

Due to the high levels of entry and the predictable court congestion in June and July (in terms of league tennis, coaching, general social play plus the tournament matches) it has been decided to move finals day from 7 July to the weekend of 29th/30th September.

 

The draw will still be made shortly and every effort should continue to be made to play matches sooner rather than later.

 

If you originally wanted to enter but did not do so due to being on holiday on 7th July please can you let Steve Ray or Geoff Redfern have an entry form by Friday 4th May or alternatively reply to this email to let us know which tournaments you would like to enter.

 

If you are on holiday on 29th/30th September then please accept our apologies but this decision is hopefully for the wider benefit of the majority of the membership.  If you have already entered the tournament, but cannot make this weekend in September, please could you let one of the above mentioned people know?
