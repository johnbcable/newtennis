Mini-Tennis Annual Tournaments 2012

Saturday 28 April 2012

Entry fee: £4

All of our mini tennis players are invited to enter our annual competition. Please join in and put into practice the skills you have learnt.

The finals will be played on the day.

Tournament Age Time and Date
Mini-Red Boys and Girls Born 2004 or later 11.00am to 1.00pm
Mini-Orange Boys and Girls Born 2003, 2004 or 2005 1.15pm to 2.45pm
Mini-Green Boys and Girls
+ Advanced Mini Orange 		Born 2002 or 2003 11.30am to 1.00pm

If you are not sure which mini-tennis colour band you belong to, please speak to Ian for guidance or go to our web site and fill in your date of birth for a quick check.

Please don't forget participants who are aged 7 or older as at 1 April 2012 must be MEMBERS of the club. Younger participants must be registered on the club data base. If you wish to know more, contact Chester Barnes on , go onto our website www.hammptontennis.org.uk, or pick up a membership application form from the tennis notice board in the club house.

Bad weather on tournament day?
If the weather is uncertain, please phone Ian (07968 451956) to check if it is still going to take place.
