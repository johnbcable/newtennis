2008 Annual Mini-Tennis Tournaments


Mini-Tennis (Orange) Tournament - Sunday 8 June, 2008

The Mini-Tennis Orange tournament contenders and their coach, Ian Poole, on court at the start of the tournament

Some really great tennis was played by our juniors at the Orange Mini Tennis Tournament held on 8 June. There were some really competitive matches and it was a pleasure to see such young children really get to grips with the game.

Congratulations to all the juniors who entered and in particular to the winner, Harry Goodworth and to the runner-up, Elliot Morter.

Thanks to our coach, Ian (the biggest one in the photo), for running the competition and also to the older juniors who acted as scorers (not an easy job!). Finally, thanks to Georgie Stanley for providing the certificates and the refreshments and of course to the families who came to support the children.
