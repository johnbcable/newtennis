Note that juniors under 16 must be accompanied by an adult. 

 

Please can you send a cheque for the tickets payable to "Hampton-in-Arden Sports Club"to Stephen Ray, 41 Old Station Road, Hampton-in-Arden, Solihull B92 0HA by Tuesday June 9th.  If you do not want to take up your allocation please let Steve know on 01675 442132 as soon as possible so that your ticket can be reallocated to members on the reserve list.  Note that tickets not paid for by Tuesday June 9th will be offered to the reserves.

 

Please note that under LTA rules the tickets are NOT transferable.  This means that if YOU cannot go you must return the ticket to the club for issue to a member drawn on the reserve list.

It is essential that you respect this rule.  The LTA do carry out spot checks and the club may forfeit future ticketing rights if there are any discrepancies.  In addition it is very unfair to those on the reserve list if you pass your tickets on. The only transfers permitted are to other family members who live in the same house.
