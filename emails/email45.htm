Hampton-In-Arden Tennis Club

The Big Racket/Geoff Redfern disco 

Saturday 15th January, 2005


Thank you to all 125 of you who supported the fund raising event last Saturday night and rocked the night away to The Big Racket and the Geoff Redfern disco.  It really was great entertainment. 

You will pleased to know that apart from giving everyone a great time, the event raised £1,418, all of which will be put into the tennis court refurbishment fund.  The fund now stands at over £32,000 - more details will follow in the beginning of season newsletter in March.

And now it is time to thank everyone who made the evening possible, especially since all of you provided your time free of charge.  As you know this is the only way that we can run a club like ours, particularly at a time when we are trying to raise funds.  Your efforts are really appreciated - I hope you enjoyed it all! 

Some names just have to be mentioned - starting with the band who have been practising every Sunday night for the past few months - so thanks to Carla, Glyn, Guy, Joe and John - you were brilliant!  Not to forget the traditional guest appearance from Peter.  I do hope that you band members appreciated all those undergarments being thrown at you during the gig! 

Next to thank is Geoff whose disco was a great follow up to The Big Racket and who had the pleasant task of entertaining everyone until the last guest wearily staggered home! 

Also to be mentioned are John and Rosalia who listened to and endured months of The Big Racket Sunday evening practice sessions at their house.  (Having telephoned one Sunday recently I know that their music room is not sound proofed!)

Finally thank you to Jenny and Jayne (the chief ticket touts!) and to all of you who helped to set up the room on Saturday afternoon, man the bar in the evening and clear up on Sunday morning - specfically Carol, Jane, Jayne, John, John, Rosalia, Sarah, Steve and Steve.

Best wishes for more tennis and social events in 2005. 

Until the next time!

Chester
