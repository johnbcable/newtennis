Junior Internal Tennis Leagues

 

 

 

Starting September 2010 Internal Tennis Leagues for:

 

Mini Red (aged 8 and under)

Mini Orange (aged 9)

Mini Green (aged 10)

Under 14

Under 16


DEADLINE for entries - 18 September
 

 

 

 

Thanks to all of you who have already let me know that you would like to take part in our new internal junior tennis leagues for all ages and abilities.

 

This is a great way to build on what you have been learning during lessons, so if you haven't already contacted me to enter, I hope you will soon.

 

When I have sufficient responses, players will be placed into suitable box leagues of between 4 - 6 players and will be given half a term to play all their matches.

 

All playing formats will be released when players are confirmed. Parents of the younger children will be in charge of organising matches and court bookings. For mini red, orange and green please liaise with the coaches to borrow the correct balls etc. It is hoped that older juniors will organise matches themselves.

 

Please only enter if you intend to fulfil the majority of the matches. Parents of mini red juniors will need to act as umpires as the younger players find it hard to concentrate on scoring and playing at the same time.

 

There will be a meeting before the start of the matches for those parents who wish to learn more about scoring etc.

 

If you are interested please email me back with the most suitable contact number. By supplying your contact number it is assumed you are happy for these details to be passed onto parents of other juniors. I would be grateful if you could let me know as soon as possible and by 18 September at the latest, so I can get the matches started as soon as possible.

 

Thank you and good luck to all entrants.

 

Ian
