Wimbledon Ticket Draw - Friday 21st May 2010

 

 

 

The draw for 36 Wimbledon ticket pairs will be made on  Friday 21st May at the Clubhouse at 7.30pm.

 

Are you: 

  1. 8 years old or over on June 21st 2010?
  2. prepared to pay between £50 and £208 for a pair of tickets?
  3. a member of British Tennis?, and
  4. a paid up member Hampton-in-Arden Tennis Club for 2010/11?

 If you have answered yes to all four questions then you are eligible to be included in the draw.

Members who ticked the box on the application form to say "yes" are shown below. If you are not on the list and you would like to be, simply reply to this email by Thursday 20th May. Don't worry if you are not sure if you are a member of British Tennis - we can ensure that you are enrolled. However you must have paid your tennis subscription before the draw takes place.

Junior members who are at least 8 years old are allowed to enter the draw. Juniors under 16 must be accompanied to Wimbledon by an adult.
