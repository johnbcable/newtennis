2008 Annual Mini-Tennis Tournaments


Mini red - Sat 7 June, 2pm
Mini orange - Sun 8 June, 1pm
Mini green - Sun 8 June, 2.30pm


All mini tennis players are invited to take part in our Annual Mini Tennis Tournaments!

This is a great opportunity to put into practice all the skills you have been developing. So, come and play tennis with your friends at the Club! There will be medals for the runners up and winners.

REMINDER: Juniors who are aged 7 or older as at 1 April 2008 must be members of the club to compete. Juniors below this age do need to be members, but should be registered on the club database.

There are four competition categories:

Mini red - GIRLS & BOYS BORN IN 2000 OR LATER
Mini orange - GIRLS & BOYS BORN IN 1999, 2000 OR 2001
Mini green - GIRLS BORN IN 1998 OR 1999
Mini green - BOYS BORN IN 1998 OR 1999

If you are not sure which mini-tennis colour band you belong to, please speak to Ian for guidance or go to our web site and fill in your date of birth for a quick check.

Please don't forget participants who are aged 7 or older as at 1 April 2008 must be MEMBERS of the club. Younger participants must be registered on the club data base. If you wish to know more contact Chester Barnes on , go onto our website www.hamptontennis.org.uk, or pick up a membership application form from the tennis notice board in the club house.

Bad weather on tournament day?

We do our very best to run scheduled tournaments despite the weather but as always our primary concern is safety for everyone using the courts. So, if it is raining heavily on the day of the tournament, the event will be cancelled or postponed. If the weather is uncertain, please phone Ian (07968 451956) to check if it is still going to take place.
