New Clubhouse Security System

from Thursday 7 December 2006
The new security system at the club will go live on Thursday 7 December.  Please would you take time to read the following notes on how the new system will work.
 
How will the new system work?
  • All adult members of the Sports Club will be issued with a flat circular key about one inch in diameter which can be attached to your key ring.
  • The key will have an expiry date of April 30 2007 (one month after the end of the subscription year). On payment of your 2007/08 subscription the expiry date will be extended to 30 April 2008.  No action will be required from you.
  • Your key will you allow you access through the outer front door at any time of the day.
  • After 9:30pm the new inner door will be locked and you will need to ring the bell to gain entry to the clubhouse. The bar staff will release the lock after checking on the video monitor behind the bar that it is safe to do so.
  • You will need to pay a £3 deposit for each key issued. This is refundable in exchange for a returned key if you should leave the Club.
  • The keys have temporary numbers written on them with a marker pen in order to help us to get them issued. The number will wear off over time but that is not a problem as the key has a permanent internal identifier that the system can read.
  • By default Squash members keys will allow access to the Squash balcony, but no other member keys will. 
  • It is very important that you keep your own key as the keys are not interchangeable between members like the VING cards were.
  • Please dispose of your old VING card as it will not work.
How will the keys be issued to you?
  • I will be at social tennis on Tuesday 5 December, Friday 8 December and Sunday 10 December to issue keys to as many members as possible.
  • If you do not get a key by Sunday 10 December please could you call at my house to collect your key as soon as possible?
  • If you are a member of the Tennis section and also the Squash and Racketball section, please would you obtain your key through the Squash section so that you automatically have access to the Squash balcony?
  • If you a Junior Member and you would like a key, please would you or one of your parents contact me? I will not be actively checking to see if you would like a key, but you are very welcome to have a key if you want one.
Thank you
