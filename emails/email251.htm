Knowle Fun Run - Call for Participants

Knowle Fun Run

May 10th, 12.00 midday.

An invitation to participate as a member of the Hampton based team - "The Good, The Bad and the Ugly"

Last year, 76 runners entered from Hampton, in teams of 4 - and were the largest team entry in this event. We raised over £4000.This year, funds raised by the team will be divided between Marie Curie Cancer Care and the Hampton Recreational Trust. As before, the Lillie Johnson Charitable Trust has kindly pledged £50 per runner. The Entry Form is in the main entrance of the Hampton in Arden Sports Club. £6.00 entry for Juniors and Secondary age, £12.00 for adults and veterans. Please hand in your fees to either Sue Kiely at 3 Meriden Rd (01675 443989) or Karen Finch at 9 Station Rd (01675 443160), or behind the Sports Club bar. Our Tee shirt colour will be blue this year. All team sponsorship is taken care of, but if you would like to download your own sponsorship form for additional sponsors, visit www.knowlefunrun.org.uk.

Please enter as soon as possible as there is a limit of 1900 runners in this event with over 600 runners registered already. - we suggest no later than 17 April.

Sue Kiely 01675 43989
