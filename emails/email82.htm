MAXI-Tennis Tournament - February and March 2006

  A very sociable way to play tennis with friends old and new, with a bit of team competition thrown in!

All full paying adult members together with the top junior members as recommended by Alan Cutts are eligible to play.  Hopefully as many people as possible will take part as each team will have at least 5 fixtures.

All eligible members have been allocated to one of the Six Nations rugby playing countries and fixtures will be played (except for half term weekend) in parallel to the Six Nations Rugby fixtures. 

Fixtures will last for approximately 90 minutes and teams will consist of 6 players (4 adults and 2 juniors) each of whom will play 2 doubles games and 2 singles games.  Each game will be won by the first player to win 10 points (using tie break rules).  As it is a team game it is the highest team score that wins the fixture.  Do you want to know about the format of the competition?  Then please click on this link and also read the attached set of rules. 

Two captains have very kindly agreed to lead and select each of the teams. So thank you to:

England - Sandy Wasse and Sarah White

France - Jo Ray and Jenny Roberts

Ireland - Lesley Cable and Clare Scott

Italy - Jayne Barnes and Rosalia Eccleston

Scotland - Yvonne Jones and Julie Williamson

Wales - Carol Grant and Jane Hodge

 Matches will be played on Sunday afternoons as follows: Feb 5, Feb 26, Mar 5, Mar 12 and Mar 19.  Finals day will be held on Saturday April 1 prior to the  Quiz.

Please let us know if you do or do not want to play in one or more of the fixtures by replying to this email by Saturday 28 January.  Your response will be passed on to the relevant captain who will get back in touch with you directly on your email or by phone.
