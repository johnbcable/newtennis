Hampton in Arden Tennis Club is 50 this year, and we are going to celebrate! We have an Anniversary Ball, commemorative merchandise and plenty of activities on the go over the celebration weekend of June 10th and June 11th, 2016. See what's on offer below.

On and off court, we have it covered! Over the celebration weekend you can browse our tennis club memorabilia, take part in an "open mic" session and round it off with some 24-hour tennis! All ages welcome. See what's on offer.
