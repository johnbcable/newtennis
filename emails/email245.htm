Hampton-In-Arden Tennis Club Court Development

 

 Tennis Court Development
will be starting on
Monday 2nd February, 2009

Work will start on Monday 2nd February to replace the tennis courts with four new courts (two tarmac and two euroclay) plus a ‘kidzone’ which is a safe play area for younger children. This is a truly exciting development and will provide top class facilities for years to come.

 

The project should take 14 weeks to complete, with the new courts available for use from early May.  Alternative facilities are in the process of being arranged for both coaching and general play.  We will let you know what arrangements have been made as soon as we have finalised them.

 

Unfortunately, there will be some disruption with part of the car park out of use to accommodate construction vehicles and equipment.  As parking will be limited we would ask that you kindly make alternative parking arrangements or walk/cycle/share lifts for drop-offs. 

 

We apologise for any inconvenience and appreciate your understanding.
