MAXI tennis 2007 - the return!

  A very sociable way to play tennis with friends old and new, with a bit of team competition thrown in!

All adult members together with the top junior members as recommended by Alan Cutts are eligible to play.  Everyone has been allocated to a team to represent one of the Rugby Six Nations Countries.  Each team will have 5 fixtures every other Sunday afternoon between January 21 and March 25. 

Fixtures last for approximately 90 minutes and teams consist of 4 adults and 2 juniors. Each team member plays 2 doubles and 2 singles games.  Games are won by the first player to win 10 points using tie break rules.  As it is a team game it is the highest team score that wins the fixture.

The way the competition has been set up, any club member regardless of standard can play and can expect to play matches against other members of an equivalent standard.

Not done MAXI-Tennis before? Want to know about the format of the competition?  Then please click on this link to further info on our web site and also check the Fixtures area to keep up to date with the scores!
