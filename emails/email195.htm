Please can you send a cheque for the tickets payable to “Hampton-in-Arden Sports Club” to Chris Barnes, 68 Meriden Road, Hampton-in-Arden by Friday June 6th.  If you do not want to take up your allocation please let me know on as soon as possible so that we can reallocate your ticket to members on the reserve list.

Please note that under LTA rules the tickets are NOT transferable.  This means that if YOU cannot go you must return the ticket to the club for issue to a member drawn on the reserve list.

It is essential that you respect this rule.  The LTA do carry out spot checks and the club may forfeit future ticketing rights if there are any discrepancies.  In addition it is very unfair to those on the reserve list if you pass your tickets on - even to members of your own family.
