Payment for tickets

This is to remind you that if you wish to take up your ticket allocation you must send a cheque made out to Hampton-in-Arden Sports Club to Chris Barnes at 68 Meriden Road, Hampton-in-Arden, Solihull B92 0BT by Friday 4th June at the latest.  If no cheque is received by Friday 4th June we will assume that you do not wish to take up your ticket allocation and the unclaimed tickets will be offered to members on the reserve list. 

 

Those shown in green have paid for their tickets and those shown in red have indicated that they do not wish to take up their ticket allocation.  No information has been received from anyone else.  As there are 5 people who have indicated that they do not wish to take up their allocation, the first 5 on the reserve list will be offered tickets in due course.  

 

Please note that there will be no further reminder to pay.  If you cannot go please could you reply to this email as soon as possible to help us?

 

Collection of tickets

Please arrange to collect you tickets from Wayman Morris.  He lives on Shadowbrook Lane directly opposite the back of the sports club with a steep in and out drive.  He will be at home between 6pm and 8pm on Monday 7th and Tuesday 8th June to enable you to collect your tickets.  If you cannot come at those times please contact him on 01675 442071.

 

Rules

The named person in draw may nominate a guest of their own choosing who does not have to be a member of the tennis club, provided that if the named person is under 16 he or she must be accompanied by an adult guest.  There is no need to let the club know who the guest is so long the person named below uses one of the tickets.

 

Please note that under LTA rules the tickets are NOT transferable.  This means that if the named member cannot go, the ticket allocation must be returned to the club to be offered to a member drawn on the reserve list in the sequence shown below.   Please note that if you do not follow this rule you run the risk of jeopardising the club's future ticket allocation.

 

Thank you for your assistance.
