Auction of Promises

It’s not too late to buy a ticket or put in a bid!

 

Saturday 15th May 2004

7:00 pm for 7:30 pm

Fentham Hall, Fentham Road

Hampton-In-Arden

It’s not too late to buy a ticket for our Auction of Promises event this Saturday night.  Maybe you are stuck for ideas for a present – we have plenty of things on offer which are not normally available.  For example, a new promise which has been added is a factory tour for two people at B.A.R Formula 1 Team, Brackley, Northants, home to Jenson Button and Takuma Sato.  It will also give you an insight into the world of Formula 1 from designing a car to the final product. 

Or maybe you would prefer something more relaxing.  There is a full body massage on offer from Rowington Health and Beauty.

Kate Wills has generously changed her dinner party promise from four to eight people.  Saves on taxis and you can’t beat great  home cooking! 

Still hungry?  There is a champagne breakfast on offer at Naughty Nick’s.  A fab start to the day!

If you can’t make the auction in person,  all is not lost - simple email a bid in.

Alternatively, if you don’t wish to bid, why not just come along and enjoy the champagne and nibbles in the company of friends.  There are ladies only and men only tables if you wish to join.

All proceeds towards the court refurbishment fund.

Thank you for your support!
