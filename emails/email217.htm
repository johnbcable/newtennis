Reminder - British Tennis Membership


The LTA have announced that in 2009, our allocation of Wimbledon tickets will be based on the number of club members who have signed up to British Tennis Membership at 30 November 2008. If you are an adult or junior member and you are not on the list of signed up members please click on the link below and help us to get a bigger allocation of Wimbledon Tickets.

www.lta.org.uk/membership.


British Tennis Membership is free to members of affiliated clubs until at least 2012. Registration takes a minute or two and within 5 minutes the person whose details appear on the form gets a confirmatory email back explaining more about the British Tennis initiative. British Tennis membership includes a broad range of benefits such as a player rating, membership card, advice from professional coaches and top players, an e-newsletter, special members-only website content, information on competitions and events and entry into (occasional) British Tennis members-only ticket draws (e.g. for Stella Artois Queens Club tickets) in addition to the Wimbledon ticket draw held via the tennis club.
