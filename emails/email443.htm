Inter-District Tennis Champions

Our Mens 1st team represented the Coventry League in the Inter District final (against Birmingham) played on Friday 28th September at the Billesley Indoor Tennis Centre ... and came away champions!.

A deservedly fantastic end to the season - twice champions in one year, and the first time in the club's history

Well done to all the Mens 1st Team squad for such an exciting and consistently good season. Onwards and upwards!
