SOLIHULL SCHOOLS TENNIS COMPETITION 2006 !

Saturday 17th and Sunday 18th June 2006
 at Solihull School, Warwick Road, Solihull
  9 am onwards

This two-day tournament is designed as a FUN tournament (many children who enter will not have entered a competition before). The tournament has been extended this year to include a 15 and over age group.

The tournament is open to all children who live or attend school in the Solihull borough. Your local school may also send out the attached leaflet to you.
