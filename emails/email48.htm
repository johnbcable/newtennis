Attention all adult male tennis club members!


The football season is nearing a conclusion and a summer of tennis is almost upon us!!!

The Coventry League season starts in the week commencing Monday 25 April 2005 and runs through until the first week of August

A full list of fixtures for all teams is now available via the club web site (//hamptontennis.org.uk)under the Fixtures menu. In addition, the full list of fixtures for the Men's 2nd and 3rd teams is attached to this email.


A first practice session for the Men's 2nd and 3rd teams will be held next Sunday 3rd April from 4pm until 6pm at the club.

Please can you let me know if you do not want to be considered for selection - some of you may also be required for 1st team action as well this year!!!

If you can't make next Sunday but you are interested in playing Men's League Tennis, let me know - if Sundays are not a great time for practice sessions, we may be able to arrange a practice during a weekday evening.

Also, try and get along to one of our Club Sessions (Tuesday evening, Friday evening or Sunday morning)- they are a friendly social gathering and will ensure that we hit the floor running for the leagues this year.
