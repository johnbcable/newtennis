Autumn 2010 Junior Coaching Programme - REMINDER

 

 

 

 

 

 

Monday 6 September to Saturday 18 December 2010

(excluding half term, week beginning 25 October)

 

Sadly summer is almost over and our coach, Ian, is planning the new term's coaching programme. It would greatly assist us if you could send your booking form and payment as soon as possible please. If you do not wish to return, please let us know by relying to this email or by letting Ian know on: 07968 451956

 

A schedule and a booking form are attached.

 

All juniors aged 7 or over on 1 April 2010 must be members of the tennis club. However, attendees who are new to the courses can play for half a term without being members whilst they decide whether tennis at Hampton is for them. For more information on joining the tennis club, please visit our web site

 

Juniors aged under 7 on 1 April 2010 have to be registered on the club database. There is no charge for this.

 

The tennis courses recommence on Monday 6 September, in line with the start of the new school year. There are 14 weeks in the course and the coaching fees are as follows:

   
1-hour lessons £70
45 minutes £56
30 minutes £42

In order to encourage prompt payment, which makes administration much easier, there are discounts as shown below for those who pay their termly fee on or before the first lesson. Conversely, there will be a surcharge for late payments not received by the 3rd lesson of term. The amounts for prompt payment and also the amounts for late payments are as follows:.


  Prompt Payment
(If you pay on/before first lesson)		 Late Payment
(If you pay after third lesson)

We look forward to seeing you back on court in September!
