ANNOUNCEMENT!

BE VERY PROUD -

YOU ARE A MEMBER OF

THE WARWICKSHIRE LTA

CLUB OF THE YEAR!

 

 
We are extremely excited and proud to announce that Hampton-in-Arden Tennis Club has been chosen as the Club of the Year by the Warwickshire Lawn Tennis Association.  This was announced at an awards ceremony sponsored by Jobson James which was held last Friday at the Botanical Gardens in Edgbaston.  Apart from receiving a beautiful glass engraved trophy we were also presented with a £1,000 cheque courtesy of the Bank of Ireland.  As there are 70 tennis clubs in Warwickshire, most of whom have more facilities than us, the win is no mean feat!
 
The award has been given to us in recognition of the great strides we have been making over recent years:
  • welcoming players of all abilities and ages
  • building our coaching programme
  • attracting top quality coaches
  • increasing our membership
  • improving playing opportunities and social events
  • planning and fund raising for replacement of the courts
 
This provides fantastic publicity for us which we can highlight in bids for funding for our new courts.
 
We would like to thank everyone who has been involved in making Hampton such a great tennis club - juniors, adults, tennis coaches, volunteers, parents of juniors, etc etc.  This award is a credit to all concerned.
 
We still have a tremendous amount of work to do in respect of the court refurbishment scheme, not to mention organising the "normal" functioning of the club, so we would like to thank you in advance for your continuing support.
 
With our best wishes for the future with Hampton-in-Arden Tennis Club.
 
The Main and Junior Committees
