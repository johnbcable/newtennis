Mens and Ladies Doubles Plate
Tournament Draws 2012

The 2012 draw for the mens and ladies doubles plate is attached. It is also on display on the tennis club noticeboard and will be posted on the web site shortly.

Deadlines - The deadlines for playing matches are as follows:

  • Box games - by Sunday October 14th
  • Semi Finals - by Sunday October 21st
  • Final - by Sunday October 28th

Rules - Please read the rules in red in the attached draw. Please note that the third set if needed will be played as a championship tie break (first to 10 points) and that you will need to put the full score of the match on the draw sheet in the clubhouse in case more than one pair win the same number of matches in a box.

Late entries - If you would like to enter either tournament and you are not yet in the draw please can you reply to this email by Sunday September 16th at the latest and we will do our best to include you. Please understand that it may not always be possible to do so.

Booking a court - You can check court availability and book courts at www.hamptontennis.org.uk by clicking on the "Book a court" link and logging on with your User ID and PIN. If you need help or you have forgotten your User ID or PIN please email support@hamptontennis.org.uk.
