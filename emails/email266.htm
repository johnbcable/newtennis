May Half Term 2009 - Junior Camps

Two camps are on offer over half term on our brand new courts!

9.30am to 3.30pm, Monday 25 May, Full or Half Day - Multisports Camp

 Juniors of all ages are invited to join this sports camp.  There will be a variety of activities including tennis, football, cricket, racket ball and table tennis. 

 The price for a full day is £14.50 (half day £8.50) for members and children aged 7 or under on 1 May 2009; £16.50 (half day £10.00) for non-members. Discounts available for family bookings - please contact Ian.

9.30am to 12.30pm Tuesday 26 May - Performance Rackets Camp 

For over 12's, or invitation only.  This tennis camp will include tournament preparation and tactical development.  There will be goal setting and attendees will be issued with a mini report card. 

The camp is open to members only and costs  £10.00.

 Don't forget: Keep your eyes on your email's and the Tennis Club web Event Diary for more events (when we list them!)

In order to ensure we have the correct staffing ratios, please prebook your place by completing the booking form below and returning it to Head Coach, Ian Poole by  Friday 22 May at the latest.
