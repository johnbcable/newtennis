Spring 2011 Junior Coaching Programme

Monday 3 January to Saturday 9 April 2011

(excluding half term, week beginning 21 February)

Many thanks to Ian and his coaching team for their hard work during the current term, particularly for their efforts during the recent harsh weather conditions. Thank you to all our juniors and parents/carers for your continuing support - continuity is important for the development of the children's tennis and is also essential for us to be able to run a year-round tennis programme. We hope you will continue to support us in 2011.

The Spring term will be starting in just a few weeks and a schedule and booking form are attached. The coaching fees including discounted and surcharged rates, are given below. In addition, we have arranged for indoor facilities at the Scout and Guide Hut and at Fentham Hall for our younger players for the first half term. The groups involved are given below. We are asking parents to contribute £1 per session towards the cost of hiring these facilities. Obviously, this is a little extra expense, but we hope you agree it is worth it to keep the younger children warm as well as fit and active.

The tennis courses recommence on Monday 3 January run for 13 weeks. The costs are as follows:

1-hour lessons £65
1-hour lessons (indoors)* £71
45-minute lessons £52
45-minute lessons (indoors)* £58
30-minute lessons £39

*An extra £6 is added as a contribution towards indoor facilities for the following red groups:

  • Monday 4-5 pm George Fentham Hall
  • Tuesday 3.45-4.30 pm George Fentham Hall
  • Wednesday 3.45-4.30 pm Scout and Guide Hut
  • Thursday 4-4.45 pm Scout and Guide Hut

As usual, in order to encourage prompt payment, which makes administration much easier, there are discounted rates as shown below for those who pay their termly fee before Christmas. Please make all cheques payable to "Ian Poole" and hand them to him or post them to 114 Castle Lane, Olton, Solihull, B92 7AN.

Chasing payment is time consuming and tedious. There will be a surcharge for late payments not received by the 3rd lesson of term.

If you are not planning to return, please can you let Ian know by the end of term in person or via mobile: 07968 451956. It is really important for Ian to know how many children will be on the programme as he needs to maintain suitable coach to player ratios.

All juniors aged 7 or over on 1 April 2010 must be members of the tennis club. However, attendees who are new to the courses can play for half a term without being members whilst they decide whether tennis at Hampton is for them. For more information on joining the tennis club, please click here.

Juniors aged under 7 on 1 April 2010 have to be on the club database. There is no charge for this.

We wish you a Very Merry Christmas and look forward to seeing you on court in 2011!
