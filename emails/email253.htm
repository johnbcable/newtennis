Calling all Juniors!

Do you want to get active during the Easter Holidays?

Juniors of all ages are welcome to join our sports camps. There will be a variety of activities including tennis, football, cricket, racket ball and table tennis.

The daily price is £14.50 for members and children aged 7 or under on 1 April 2009. The price is £19.50 for non-members.

There is a reduced rate for booking all 3 days in a week - £34.50 for members and children aged 7 and under; and £44.50 for non-members.

In order to ensure we have the correct staffing ratios, please prebook your place by completing the booking form below and returning it to Head Coach, Ian Poole by 25 March at the latest.
